Jerwin Ancajas Biography

Jerwin Juntilla Ancajas was born on January 1, 1992. He is 31 years old and has the Capricorn sun sign. He was born and raised in Panabo, a Davao City suburb in the Philippines.

Jesar Ancajas, Jerwin’s brother, introduced him to boxing in the second grade. Before turning pro in 2009, he won several national championships, including a gold medal at the Palarong Pambansa at the age of 17. According to his Facebook profile, he attended Panabo National High School.

Zac Efron and Jeremy Allen White lead the biographical sports film, which premiered in US theaters nationwide on Friday, December 22, 2023.

What is The Iron Claw based on?

The Iron Claw chronicles the lives of the Von Erich professional wrestling family.

Hailing from Texas, the clan's actual surname is Adkisson but it was later changed for the purpose of Jack Adkisson's wrestling career as Fritz Von Erich.

During the 1960s, Fritz gained a following for competing in World Class Champion Wrestling.

The three-time world champion opened the door for his sons to follow in his footsteps.

Kevin, David, Kerry, Mike, and Chris became wrestlers as well.

The latter three wrestlers died by suicide and David Von Erich died from Acute Enteritis, which leaves Kevin as the last surviving of his brothers.

Kevin and his father Fritz are associated with popularizing "the iron claw" wrestling move.

The move involves the user dramatically incapacitating their opponent by grabbing them by their head.

In 2009, the dynasty of wrestlers was inducted into the WWE Hall of Fame.

How can I watch The Iron Claw?

On December 22, 2023, The Iron Claw was released in theaters nationwide.

It was distributed by A24, an American independent entertainment company.

The film is scheduled to be released on February 9, 2024, in the United Kingdom.

It will be distributed by Lionsgate UK, a British subsidiary of the American-Canadian global mass media and entertainment organization.

As of December 23, 2023, there is no word yet on when The Iron Claw will be released on digital platforms and streaming services.

The real Kevin Von Erich has expressed his approval of Zac's portrayal of him.

"I haven’t spoken to him, but my daughters love him, and they think he’s the right guy to do it," he told TMZ.

"He looks really good and muscular! He must be really working hard."

I tried four different anti chafing shorts to see if any could really stop the dreaded chub rub - he

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Saturday, March 23, 2024

ANYONE who's experienced chub rub in the summer knows just how painful it can really be.

That's why one fashion fan decided to put four different anti chafing shorts to the test to see once and for all which are the best.

Carrie Dayton bought shorts from brands including Snag, Spanx and Skims - and the best of the bunch might surprise you.

"Over the years I've has a lot of trial and error when it comes to trying different products, different shorts, different remedies or just preventions in general," the fashion lover explained.

To start her haul, she first tried on a £30 pair of shorts from Thigh Society.

Although the white shorts took longer than expected to arrive, they were well worth the wait.

"They're almost like super stretchy, comfy, very thick tights," Carrie said.

The shorts didn't move, roll up and slip down when she moved either, which was a huge bonus.

Next, Carrie tried on the Stay Cool Chub Rub Shorts from Snag, which cost just £8.69.

Despite the much lower price point, the material and quality really impressed the fashion fan, who loved the fit.

Because of the more breathable material, Carrie loved the shorts for the hotter weather but did worry they'd move more while walking around.

The Spanx shorts were the next pair she put to the test, and she didn't have high hopes to begin with.

"I hate the feeling of this waistband, I'm just being honest," Carrie said.

She did, however, love that the gusset had extra lining and thought they'd be good if you wanted something to shop chafing that also sucks you in, like shapewear.

She continued: "It does feel more than any other pair like they would roll up because there's no seam."

Finally, Carried tried on the Skims Soft Smooth Shorts, and even though the website only had the black colour in stock, she loved the feel of the fabric, and the fit was even better.

"These are perfect. They're exactly what I wanted. These are one of the best.

"I love the length, I feel like you could almost double these as bike shorts because they're that opaque," she said.

I'm Not Gay! It's All For Ratings!

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 10, 2024

If you watched Vanderpump Rules Season 3 Episode 11, then you know why the question of Jax Taylor’s sexuality is on everyone’s minds this week.

Rumors that Jax is gay have surfaced on the show before, but for the first time, producers Tom Sandoval actually introduced one of Jax’s alleged former hook-up partners to the rest of the cast.

John Walters has been cagey about his past with Jax, and he has hasn’t stated outright that they had sex (though he admits they "slept together"), but it seems pretty obvious that Taylor has, indeed, dabbled with dudes.

Not that there’s anything wrong with that! But Jax, being the compulsive liar that he is, has of course ramped up his efforts to prove to the world that he’s straight as an arrow that platonically shares a bed with his bros.

Jax has reportedly been telling friends that "the network set that up" because execs "want things to look a certain way."

If the scene was staged – as Jax claims – it would have required some solid acting from Taylor and company. Frankly, we don’t think they would’ve been up to the task. And it seems we’re not alone:

On an episode of Watch What Happens Live that aired after the now-infamous Jax and John scene, viewers were asked if they think Taylor and Walters were more than just friends.

A whopping 96% responded that they believe Jax swings both ways. 

Never one to miss an opportunity to sling some BS, Taylor took to Twitter to claim that he’s straighter than John Wayne arm-wrestling Clint Eastwood at the Republican National Convention…he just likes to give his BFFs the occasional peck on the cheek:

"Well if giving your boy a kiss is gay then they are right!!" Jax tweeted in response to the Bravo poll. "I don’t give a sh-t what people say!"

You seem so tense, Jax! We recommend you get one of your fellow male models to oil you up and give you a massage, ASAP!

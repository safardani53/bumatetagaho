Primark fans scramble to get their hands on the new-in Chlo dupe sandals that look almost identica

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, February 24, 2024

A PRIMARK super fan has taken to social media to show off the new-in sandals that you will want to snap up for Spring.

We all love Primark for their cheap clothes, brilliant homeware items and make-up dupes, but it’s the latest pair of sandals that have left shoppers open-mouthed.

If you’re a fan of designer clobber but can’t justify the hefty prices, particularly in the current cost-of-living crisis, you’ll want to head down to the high-street as quickly as you can, as Primark have left us very impressed, yet again. 

Posting on TikTok under the username @primarkxo, the Primark-obsessed shopper spotted a brand-new pair of chic sliders.

Not only are the summer shoes stunning, but they’re also a dupe for the Chloé woody crocheted slides. 

The comfy mules are made from white woven fabric with two crossover straps and chic squared toes.

They can be worn with dresses and jeans and are the perfect spring buy.

They're a very versatile shoe - and are great for holidays or for running errands too.

The social media user said: “New in Primark”, as she showed off her fabulous find. 

The Primark pair are just £9, a whopping £356 cheaper than the original Chloé pair, which have a hefty price tag of £365.

But if you couldn't imagine spending such an eye-watering amount on a pair of shoes, the Primark pair are a brilliant dupe, which look almost identical.

But you’ll need to be quick if you want to get your hands on these, as Primark fans are scrambling to nab a pair before they sell out.

And once something gets big on TikTok, it tends to sell out fast. 

The social media clip has clearly impressed many, as it was posted yesterday, but has already amassed a whopping 118.4k views.

It has 4,423 likes, 13 comments and 216 shares.

Many shoppers tagged their friends in the comments to flag up the new-in bargain buy.

Others were eager to get their hands on these cheap shoes. 

One person said: “I need the shoes.”

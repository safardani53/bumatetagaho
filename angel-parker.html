Angel Parker Biography

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, May 25, 2024

On 10th of August 2002, Angel got married to Eric Nenninger. Eric Nenninger is an actor from United States and he is famous for his role in the movie Jeepers Creepers II. As a loving spouse, they have been parents twice. It’s been more than a decade they have been married and there is no any rumors about her extra affairs and boyfriend.

Angel was raised in south Orange County. There she attended some high school and she finally completed her high school graduation at the age of 16. After that she joined American Academy of Dramatic Arts. She also attended British American Drama Academy for several years. She grew up with big dreams in acting and she was finally able to accomplish that dream in her late teens. In 2000, she starred the role of Veronica in the TV series Angel. In 2008, she appeared in the TV series like Criminal Minds, The Young and the Restless and ER. She made her feature film debut in 2012 starring the role of Joel’s Mother in the movie Free Samples. She has been earning lucrative amount of salary and her net worth is $1 million.

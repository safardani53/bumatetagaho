The Best Celebrity Family Halloween Costumes of All Time

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

Melissa Rycroft and Tye Strickland's Flintstones Costume

Melissa Rycroft, Tye Strickland and their daughter Ava traded in the Halloween "boos" for "yabba dabba doos" when they dressed as the Flinstones family in 2011.

Jessica Alba and Cash Warren's Incredibles Costume

Super powers, spooks and spandex! Jessica Alba, Cash Warren and their daughter sported Incredibles costumes for Halloween in 2012.

Tom Brady and Gisele Bündchen's Dragon Costume

Tom Brady, Gisele Bündchen and their kids rocked fire 'fits when they sported dragon costumes on Halloween in 2014.

"That's what happens when you let your 4-year-old choose the family halloween costumes," Gisele captioned her Instagram photo.

Pink and Carey Hart's E.T. Costume

Pink and Carey Hart's Halloween costume was out of this world! The couple and their daughter Willow dressed as characters from the beloved film, E.T. the Extra-Terrestrial, in 2015.

Alyssa Milano and David Bugliari's Despicable Me Costume

Alyssa Milano, David Bugliari and their kids' Halloween costume was spot on in 2015. The brood dressed as characters from 2010's Despicable Me (complete with visiting Minion).

Kourtney Kardashian's Family Superhero Costume

Meet the League of Extraordinary Kardashians! Kourtney Kardashian posed as Wonder Woman, with her oldest son, Mason, and daughter, Penelope, beside her, their chests emblazoned with lightning bolts. Her youngest son, Reign, also sported a red cape.

(Kourtney's niece North West bucked the trend, opting to go as a unicorn.) Kardashian's caption? "Squad."

Shakira and Gerard Piqué's Topo Gigio Costume

Shakira and now-ex Gerard Piqué's kids seemingly took the lead on Halloween in 2015 when the whole gang dressed as the friendly mice from the Italian puppet show, Topo Gigio.

Cindy Crawford and Rande Gerber's Punk Rock Costume

Eyeliner, mascara, studs... check! Cindy Crawford, Rande Gerber and their kids, Kaia and Presley, didn't hold back when they donned punk rock ensembles on Halloween in 2016.

Mario Lopez and Courtney Lopez's Cowboy Costume

They're giving big yeehaw energy! Mario and Courtney Lopez wrangled up their brood on Halloween in 2016, dressing in the cutest western garb — boots, hat, horse and all!

Nick Cannon and Son Moroccan's Super Mario Costume

Nick Cannon and his son Moroccan dressed as everyone's favorite Italian plumbers when they sported Mario and Luigi costumes on Halloween in 2016.

Meanwhile, now-ex Mariah Carey and daughter Monroe opted for something different — a devil and princess, respectively.

Tamera Mowry and Adam Housley's Ghostbusters Costume

Who ya gonna call? The Housleys! Tamera Mowry and Adam Housely dressed their family up as characters from Ghostbusters on Halloween in 2016 — the holiday that falls just days before their son Aden's birthday!

"Thanks to all our family and friends who came to Aden's spooky 4th b day party. Ariah didn't want to wear her hat and Aden did not want a costume," Tamera wrote on Instagram.

Ryan Lochte and Kayla Lochte's Aladdin Costume

Ryan and Kayla Lochte brought the Halloween magic — and the monkey business — when they dressed up as Aladdin and Jasmine in 2017, complete with an Abu-costumed baby Caiden, who celebrated his first Halloween that year.

Eric Decker and Daughter Vivian's Moana Costume

No monsters, only mahalos! For Halloween in 2017, Eric Decker and his daughter Vivian dressed as Disney's Hawaiian power duo: Moana and Maui!

Tiffani Thiessen and Brady Smith's Oatmeal Raisin Cookie Costume

Despite their costumes, Halloween 2017 looked far from crumby for Tiffani Thiessen's family who whipped up the sweetest costume.

While the actress dressed as the Sun-Maid Raisin girl and her husband Brady Smith dressed as Quaker Oats, their little kiddos dressed as the product of the two: Oatmeal raisin cookies!

Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel's Toy Story Costume

It was a Boos-Lightyear kind of vibe for Justin Timberlake and his family when they dressed as the characters from Toy Story on Halloween in 2017.

The singer donned the beloved Buzz Lightyear costume, while Jessica Biel dressed as Jessie and their son Silas dressed as Woody!

Jaime King and Kyle Newman's Power Rangers Costume

Fighting crime and collecting candy at the same time! Jaime King, her now-ex Kyle Newman and their squad were all in when they dressed as Power Rangers on Halloween in 2017.

Katherine Heigl and Josh Kelley's Russian Nesting Doll Costume

Katherine Heigl, Josh Kelley and their kids dressed as Russian nesting dolls for Halloween in 2017.

"Family themed costumes may be a new tradition... at least as long as I can talk my kids into it!" she wrote on Instagram.

Alyssa Milano and Her Daughter's Daisy Duck Costume

Alyssa Milano and her chick! The mommy-daughter duo donned the same Halloween costume in 2017, dressing in the coziest-looking Daisy Duck onsies for a fun-filled day of trick or treating.

Justin Timberlake and Jessica Biel's Lego Movie Costume

No missing pieces here! Justin Timberlake, Jessica Biel and son Silas were made for Halloween, rocking the coolest costumes from The Lego Movie.

Kylie Jenner and Daughter Stormi's Thunder and Lightening Costumes

Seemingly a tribute to Kylie Jenner's baby girl's name Stormi, the mother-daughter duo dressed as thunder and lightning on Halloween in 2018.

Mario Lopez's Cobra Kai Costume

Mario Lopez and his kids showed no mercy on Halloween in 2019. Rather than kicking it at the Cobra Kai dojo, the karate-clad family opted for trick or treating instead!

Khloé Kardashian and Daughter True's 101 Dalmatians Costume

Spot on! Khloé Kardashian and her daughter True donned a black-and-white ensemble for their Halloween costume in 2019. Kardashian channeled a fashionable Cruella de Vil, while her little girl resembled the cutest Dalmatian pup!

Neil Patrick Harris and David Burtka's Willy Wonka Costume

Since Halloween is all about the candy, Neil Patrick Harris and his family had the right idea when they dressed as characters from Willy Wonka & the Chocolate Factory in 2020.

Jade Liz Roper and Son Brooks' Space Costume

Halloween was a blast for Jade Liz Roper, her son Brooks and soon-to-be-born Reed when they opted for space-themed costumes in 2020.

Jana Kramer's Superhero Costume

Monsters didn't stand a chance against Jana Kramer and her superhero family on Halloween in 2021! The singer's daughter, Jolie, and son, Jace, looked invincible (and cute) dressed as Wonder Woman and Superman.

Tiffani Thiessen and Brady Smith's Harry Potter Costume

"Happy Potterween!" Tiffani Thiessen captioned this shot of her family in their 2021 Halloween costume, complete with husband Brady Smith as Hagrid, daughter Harper as Hermione and son Holt as Harry Potter.

Ashley Tisdale and Christopher French's Where the Wild Things Are Costume

There's nothing scary about these monsters! Ashley Tisdale and her fam dressed as the cutest, little furry creatures from Where the Wild Things Are on Halloween in 2021.

Kate Upton and Justin Verlander's Monsters, Inc. Costume

Boo! Kate Upton and her husband Justin Verlander channeled master scarers Mike and Sully from Monsters, Inc., while daughter Genevieve went as the adorable Boo on Halloween in 2021.

Khloe Kardashian and Tatum's Cow Costume

Khloe Kardashian and son Tatum looked udderly adorable during a 2023 Halloween party with the family.

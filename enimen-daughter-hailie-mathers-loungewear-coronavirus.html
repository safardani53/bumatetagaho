Eminems daughter Hailie Mathers shows off sweatpants and slippers lounge look as she hangs at hom

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, June 16, 2024

EMINEM'S daughter, Hailie Jade Mathers, showed off her loungewear as she hung out at home with her dog, Lottie.

The 23-year-old shared her look for her seventh day in self-isolation due to the COVID-19 pandemic.

The recent Michigan State graduate shared a mirror selfie during her seventh day in self-isolation.

Along with the picture, she urged her followers to stay home: "self-isolation day....7? I want to hang out with my friends as bad as Lottie wants to go outside & I refuse to put on full outfits to take pictures⁣.
⁣"But seriously it’s so important to stay safe and healthy & social distance/stay home right now!

"The sooner everyone is on board, the sooner we can stop the spread of this virus & get back to as normal as possible!"

In the picture, she showed off her full-face of makeup, a clean manicure and fresh curls.

She wore a black leather jacket with a graphic tee underneath paired with white sweatpants and pink slippers.

Her dog, Lottie, was in the back staring out the window.

Just one day ago, her father praised her for finishing her college education.

The rapper revealed he couldn't be happier his daughter, 23, who he shares with ex-wife Kimberly Scott, finished her studies at Michigan State University alongside her long-time boyfriend, Evan McClintock, in 2018.

Appearing on Mike Tyson’s Hot Boxin' podcast, the 47-year-old hitmaker was surprisingly candid about his personal life and happily discussed seeing his little girl grow up.

The notoriously private star shared: "Hailie is 23, no babies, she has a boyfriend, but she’s doing good.

"She's made me proud for sure, she's graduated from college."

The rapper is also dad to Whitney, Kim's daughter from a previous relationship who he adopted, and her niece Alaina.

Hailie is in a long-term relationship with Evan, who she reportedly met while studying at Michigan State University.

The couple are believed to have started dating in 2016 while Hailie studied psychology and Evan economics.

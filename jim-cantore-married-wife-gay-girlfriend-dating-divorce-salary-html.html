Is Jim Cantore Married After Divorce With Her Ex-Wife?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, October 3, 2024

Meteorologist Jim Cantore, who was all over the news in September 2022 for nearly being blown away and hit by a tree branch during his coverage of Hurricane Ian, has had quite a turbulent personal life. He went through a divorce and has been the epicenter of a few dating rumors. The father of two children has not succeeded in his marital life and carries multiple dating affairs rumors in his dating history. Well, we will be broadcasting his dating to married life in fine taste!

Jim Cantore is mainly known as an on-air personality who reports severe weather events for The Weather Channel. His lauded for his ability to turn complex news into digestible forms for average viewers. Adding to that, his live field coverage of major Hurricanes such as Katrina, Gustav, Isabel, Andrew, Floyd, Mitch, Laura, Sandy, Irene, Dorlan, and Ian has brought immense viewership to the channel.

Jim received the 2002 NOAA-David S. Johnson Award for his original method of using environmental satellite technology, and he also holds the AMS Television Seal of Approval.

Who is Jim Cantore dating now?

Despite his failed married life and his several dating rumors, some of his fans speculated him to be gay because he remained silent about his personal life for a long time in the past.

But in fact, he is currently in a relationship with CNN freelance meteorologist Britley Ritz.

However, it is not known whether they are married or just dating. From their social media posts, the pair like to visit each other families during Holidays and important events. The pair seem to have a great bond and love towards each other circles. 

In his Instagram posts, Cantore likes to call Ritz his "better half." 

Who is Jim Cantore's ex-wife?

Jim was a married man who tied the knot with Tamara Cantore, the former executive at The Weather Channel, in 1990.

The former couple first met when they were working on The Weather Channel back in the 1980s. The workmates soon took their dating life to marriage after dating for a while.

Tamara was reportedly handling PR for Cantore for the channel. They officially divorced in 2006.

How many kids does Jim Cantore have?

Jim and his then-wife Tamara welcomed their first child, a daughter named Christina, in 1993. Later in 1995, Tamara gave birth to a son named Ben.

What led to Jim Cantore and his Wife's Divorce?

As the couple was raising the kids, Tamara was diagnosed with the neurological disease Parkinson’s Disease, and both their children suffered from Fragile X syndrome, a genetic condition that results in both mental (intellectual and behavioral) and physical disability. 

Consequently, the couple's marital relationship suffered. Ultimately, they got divorced in 2009. However, Jim still shares the same love for his children and spends time with his daughter and son, as per his social media.

"At first I was pissed. Once I accepted that some things are going to be easy and some aren't, it got better," he told Popular Mechanics in 2015. 

Also, he performs charitable works for the victims affected by the disease that hit his ex-wife and his children through the Fragile X Research Foundation and the Parkinson’s Unity Walk.

Who are Jim Cantore's ex-girlfriends?

After the separation, Jim was dating CNN news producer and journalist Andrea Butera, @andreabutera. The duo started dating in 2010. The couple then kept their relationship low-key and only featured one another on their respective social media accounts in some occasions, exhibiting close bonds. Still, they never confirmed their dating status officially. 

One such instance was in November 2017, when Jim uploaded a picture of him and  Andrea walking around Soulfully Good Cafe on his Instagram.

Similarly, Andrea Instagrammed a picture with Jim enjoying lunch at Bear River Bar & Grill in Steamboat Springs in Colorado. It looked like they were enjoying their intimacy at the time.

After 2019, both Andrea and Jim's social media profiles didn't feature one another, which seemingly suggested to fans that their relationship had ended.

Where is Jim Cantore Right Now?

James M. Cantore was born on February 16, 1964, in Beacon Falls, Connecticut. Following his graduation from Lyndon State College in 1986, Jim was given his first job by The Weather Channel, and he has been working there ever since.

The 59-year-old meteorologist is especially recognized for his ability to break down complex weather events into terms that average viewers can easily understand, which is why he's the guy when it comes to reporting severe weather events.  

Despite the fact that his best-known agenda is his live field coverage of major meteorological events like Hurricane Katrina, Rita, Mitch, Irene, and Matthew, Jim has his contributions spread out beyond weather reporting.

Having reported events like the Winter X Games, Space Shuttle Discovery launch, NFL games, and PGA tournaments, Jim's early works were developed in the fan-favorite Fall Foliage Forecast.

The risk-taking meteorologist also features at the start of the ECHL's Stockton Thunder entrance video and serves as the narrator on Storm Stories on TWC. He also filled in occasionally for AI Rocker on The Today Show in 2008. 

What is Jim Cantore's Net Worth? 

According to Payscale, the average annual salary of an American meteorologist is $51K. Being an experienced weathercaster, Jim earns an annual salary above average, precisely $1.5 million, contributing to his net worth of $4.5 million, according to Celebritynetworth.

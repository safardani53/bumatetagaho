Celebs Wearing Pop Culture Halloween Costumes

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 18, 2024

2021: Drew Barrymore as her 'Scream' Character

The talk show host went as her former self – her iconic character from the first Scream movie, to be exact – for Halloween in 2021.

2021: Jamie Leigh Curtis as Janet Leigh

At the Halloween Kills premiere in 2021, the actress channeled her mother, Janet Leigh, from her iconic role as Marion Crane in Psycho.

2021: Judy Greer as Annie Hall

Greer was the spitting image of Diane Keaton dressed as the titular star of Annie Hall in 2021.

2021: LeBron James as Freddy Krueger

"SWEET DREAMS," James captioned an Instagram post of the NBA star dressed as Freddy Krueger from A Nightmare on Elm Street.

2021: Olivia Rodrigo as Elvira Hancock in 'Scarface'

In 2021, the singer transformed into Elvira Hancock, Michelle Pfeiffer's character from Scarface, with a short blonde bob and blue dress.

2021: Ariana Grande and Dalton Gomez as 'Little Shop of Horrors' characters

For the pair's second couples costume, Grande and Gomez dressed up as Audrey and Seymour from Little Shop of Horrors.

2021: Channing Tatum and Zoë Kravitz as 'Taxi Driver' characters

The couple celebrated their first Halloween together in 2021 with costumes from the classic film Taxi Driver.

2020: Halsey as 'Corpse Bride'

The singer underwent a dramatic transformation inspired by the Tim Burton film Corpse Bride in 2020.

2020: January Jones as Miss Hannigan from 'Annie'

Just stick out your chin and grin ... at the star's pitch-perfect Miss Hannigan from Annie.

2020: Courteney Cox as her 'Scream 3' Character

The star took scary to a whole new level when she recreated her infamous bangs from the Scream 3 movie.

2020: The Weeknd as the Nutty Professor

The singer was all smiles in a spot-on Nutty Professor costume in 2020.

2020: Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson as Mufasa

A lion may be the King of the Jungle, but The Rock's version of Mufasa didn't quite fit in 2020.

2020: Kelly Ripa as Moira Rose

The Live with Kelly and Ryan host channeled Catherine O'Hara's Schitt's Creek character for Halloween 2020 – wig and all.

2020: Tamron Hall as Diana Ross

In 2020, the talk show host paid homage to the legendary star's Live in Central Park special.

2019: Kylie Jenner as 'The Little Mermaid'

Jenner's Disney-inspired ensemble in 2019 included a pearl-and-seashell bra, a green sequined skirt and a long red wig.

2019: Hoda Kotb as John Travolta from 'Saturday Night Fever'

On Today in 2019, Kotb brought out her disco moves as John Travolta in Saturday Night Fever.

2018: Harry Styles as Elton John

All smiles here! Styles recreated John's iconic Los Angeles Dodgers getup for the Casamigos Halloween party in 2018.

2018: Kylie Jenner and friends as Fanta

Don't you wanna? Jenner and friends celebrated Halloween 2018 as the brightly colored Fanta soft drink crew.

2018: Jessica Simpson and Eric Johnson as 'Twins'

Simpson and her husband looked identical – minus the height difference - as Danny DeVito and Arnold Schwarzenegger, respectively, from the movie Twins.

2018: Kristen Bell as Elsa

She may be best known as the voice behind Frozen's Anna, but for Halloween 2018, the actress dressed up as Anna's sister, Elsa.

2018: Lupita Nyong'o as Dionne from 'Clueless'

Another guest at Klum's 2018 Halloween bash, Nyong'o threw it back to the '90s – giant cellphone and all – as Dionne from Clueless.

2018: Gwyneth Paltrow and Brad Falchuk as Ally and Jack from 'A Star is Born'

For Halloween 2018, the Goop founder and her hubby donned leather and a cowboy hat as Ally and Jack Maine from A Star Is Born.

2018: Chrissy Teigan and John Legend as Queen Elizabeth and Prince Philip

The celebrity couple channeled British royalty in 2018, dressing up as Queen Elizabeth and Prince Philip, who died on Sept. 8, 2022, and April 9, 2021, respectively.

2017: Lauren Conrad as Cruella de Vil

"If she doesn't scare you no evil thing will," Conrad, decked out as Cruella de Vil, captioned an Instagram post in 2017.

2017: Lady Gaga as Edward Scissorhands

The pop superstar was almost unrecognizable in her 2017 Edward Scissorhands costume. "Would you like a haircut!" Gaga wrote on Instagram.

2017: LeBron James as Pennywise

The basketball superstar went from making fans cheer to making them scream when he donned a Pennywise costume in 2017. Creepy!

2016: Nina Dobrev as Ryan Lochte

"Had a blast in Rio. Oh, and we also won some Gold Medals. #GoUSA #RyanLochte #JimmyFeigen," Dobrev captioned a photo of herself as Ryan Lochte and a pal dressed as Olympian Jimmy Feigen, who also rocked silver locks during the 2016 Rio Games.

2016: Amy Schumer as Dustin from 'Stranger Things'

While Stranger Things was the inspiration behind many costumes in 2016, Schumer and then-boyfriend Ben Hanisch went all out to dress as Dustin and Eleven, respectively — and it's safe to say they nailed their looks.

2016: Bette Midler as her 'Hocus Pocus' character

In case you had any doubt, Midler won Halloween — hands down — in 2016 when she dressed up as her beloved Hocus Pocus character, Winifred Sanderson.

Iggy Azalea as Marlon Wayans in 'White Chicks'

The rapper poked fun at an Internet meme comparing her to Wayans in White Chicks, dressing up as the character in question (an FBI agent undercover as a socialite). "Bye haterzzzz," she captioned her Instagram video.

Kendall Jenner and Cara Delevingne as Mario and Luigi

Jenner was the Mario to Delevingne's Luigi in her silly Instagram snapshot.

2014: Maria Menounos as Kelly Kapowski

The TV host paid homage to Saved by the Bell's Kelly Kapowski right down to the teased bangs and the varsity jacket at a party in 2014. Though we don't remember the storyline about her romance with Captain Jack Sparrow …

2014: Colton Haynes as Princess Fiona

The Arrow actor went undercover as Shrek's Princess Fiona while attending Matthew Morrison's Halloween bash in 2014.

2014: Chace Crawford as 'Napoleon Dynamite's Uncle Rico

Anyone up for a game of catch? The actor took it back to Napoleon Dynamite as Uncle Rico in 2014.

2014: Paris Hilton as Minnie Mouse

The socialite proved Minnie Mouse can, in fact, be sexy.

2014: Ed Sheeran as Austin Powers

Groovy, baby! Always one to have fun on Halloween (proof: the singer once dressed up as killer doll Chuckie), Sheeran let his love of all things '70s run wild with his Austin Powers costume in 2014.

2014: Sarah Hyland as Annie

Fun fact: Hyland and singer-songwriter Katie Welch put together these Annie-inspired costumes in 10 minutes. "Yes, we just happen to own things that work for Annie," shared the Modern Family star.

2014: Kelly Osbourne as Gwyneth Paltro in 'The Royal Tenenbaums'

"I hope I did you justice, Gwyneth Paltrow!" Osbourne captioned her Halloween Instagram snapshot of the homage to The Royal Tenenbaums' Margot.

2013: Mindy Kaling as Captain Hook

"Should I make this my new Tinder profile pic?" asked Kaling of her Captain Hook get-up. We'd swipe right for sure.

2013: Chris Colfer as R2-D2

The Glee star took sexy to a new level when debuting his R2-D2 costume on Instagram in 2013. "I decided to go as a character whose sex appeal is always ignored," the actor wrote.

2013: Naya Rivera as Carmen Sandiego

"Just doing my part to teach the kids geography," the late Rivera said of her choice to dress as Carmen Sandiego for Halloween in 2013.

2013: Lance Bass as Garth from 'Wayne's World'

For an easy Halloween costume, channel Wayne's World's Garth and Wayne, as Bass and friend Jai Rodriguez did, respectively, with band tees, plaid shirts and trucker hats.

2013: Gisele Bündchen and Tom Brady as 'The Wizard of Oz'

In 2013, the cutest couple costume award went to this gorgeous pair, who dressed up as characters from The Wizard of Oz.

2013: The Burtka-Harris Family as 'Alice in Wonderland'

Burkta is all about themed costumes – he came up with the family's Alice in Wonderland-inspired ensemble himself.

2012: Emma Roberts as Julia Roberts in 'Pretty Woman'

The Scream Queens star paid homage to aunt Julia Roberts' character Vivian in Pretty Woman. And she totally won Halloween.

2012: Katy Perry as Jane from 'Daria'

Perry nailed teenage angst with her portrayal of best friend Jane from MTV's cult hit Daria.

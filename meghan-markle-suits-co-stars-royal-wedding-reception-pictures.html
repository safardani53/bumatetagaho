Meghan Markles Suits co-stars reveal behind-the-scene pictures from grounds of lavish wedding rec

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

THE cast of Suits have revealed behind-the-scene pictures from their former co-star Meghan Markle's wedding reception.

Gabriel Macht, Patrick J. Adams, Rick Hoffman, Sarah Rafferty and Gina Torres were all there to celebrate her big day after flying over from America to see her wed Prince Harry.

In a sweet post sharing a picture of him and his wife at the wedding, Gabriel said: "Congrats to Meghan and Prince Harry on your nuptials.

"Enjoyed meeting many of your family and friends and an incredible honour to be a part of the whole celebration.

"May your journey together be full of love and children. Cheers!"

The American actors were all invited to the Royal Wedding after-party last night at Frogmore House.

After watching Meghan say "I will" to her beau, Patrick tweeted a fan to jokingly say: "If you find me, let me know, because I'm still not really sure where I am or what just happened."

Patrick arrived with his wife, Pretty Little Liars star Troian Bellisario to the star-studded ceremony yesterday.

He was there to support on-screen wife Meghan say her vows as she became a member of the royal family.

How the Royal Wedding has unfolded so far...

After a horse-drawn carriage procession through Windsor, the newlyweds celebrated their marriage with friends at a wedding reception.

They arrived at it in an open-top electric sports car before partying the night away with the likes of Serena Williams, Priyanka Chopra and George Clooney.

The night before the wedding, the Suits cast enjoyed a meal together in London.

Meghan Markle's Suits co-star Rick Hoffman explains why he had an unimpressed face at her wedding

In a touching message to his former on-screen wife, Patrick wrote: "Meghan – wherever you are – we are so grateful to be here to watch you both take this monumental step together. Love deeply and live well. #RoyalWedding.”

Meanwhile Rick, who plays sarcastic lawyer Louis Litt in the hit US show,  took to his Instagram page after the wedding to explain what had been going on in the congregation.

It's after viewers poked fun at him for his unimpressed face while watching the ceremony.

Newlyweds Prince Harry and Meghan Markle leave Windsor Castle for their evening reception at Frogmore House

Wearing a casual top and baseball cap in a post-wedding video, he told fans: "So to answer any questions about the weird face that was made at the wedding, imagine sitting next to people to your right - not knowing which person it was - that has halitosis.

"Um. Hence the face. That is apparently all over the place.

"So it's as simple as that, Charlie. Love you guys. Yah. Halitosis. Disgusting."

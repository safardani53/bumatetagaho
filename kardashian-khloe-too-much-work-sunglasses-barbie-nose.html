Kardashian fans rip Khloe for getting 'too much work done' as her sunglasses slide off face due to '

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, March 11, 2024

KHLOE Kardashian was slammed by fans for getting "too much work done" as they claimed she had a "tiny Barbie nose."

The reality star has received flack from online trolls over her changing appearance.

Khloe sparked chatter from Reddit users after she stepped out wearing uniquely designed sunglasses.

The 38-year-old donned the oversized square frames with her blonde hair slicked back into a low bun.

She wore large round earrings and a black button-up in the upper body snapshot.

The look, however, caused some to zero in on her facial features as a slew of critics commented on her nose beneath the frames.

The original poster wrote in the caption: "Khloe’s 'Barbie nose' doesn’t compliment her facial features."

While other commenters agreed, with one writing: "Reminds me of a shark’s nose, poor thing."

Another pointed out her seemingly altered nose, noting: "Pretty soon she won’t even be able to wear glasses."

Another person wasn't fond of her choice of accessories, adding: "can't stand how she's always hung her sunglasses off the tip of her nose too. just makes it worse."

A fourth echoed the statement, chiming in: "that thing looks weighed tf down by those sunglasses."

Others reflected on how she previously appeared in photos, claiming that they preferred her older look.

"I liked her face pre nose job, her nose was fine!," one remarked.

As another sympathized with the star, commenting: "I just wish she could see herself as she is, a perfectly good looking woman, and stop with the surgeries and tanning." 

Khloe's physical transformation has been a topic of conversation on social media lately, as fans grow worried that she's overdoing it.

The mom-of-one was accused on multiple occasions of altering her photos online and criticized for her drastic weight loss.

NOT NATURAL

Over the weekend, fans called her out for looking almost unrecognizable while attending her sister Kim's party in Miami to promote the SKIMS founder's new line.

After she posted numerous pics of her rocking a tight blue dress with sheer cutouts throughout, some thought she looked unnatural.

Noting that the photo was edited, one fan commented: "She’s absolutely gorgeous but I just wish she looked a little less..computer generated? Like she doesn’t look like a real human sometimes on her ig, whereas pap shots she looks real and still gorg."

Another added: "She should look great she’s Facetuned to death."

A third chimed in: "Does she look like this in the pap pics? No… Does she look amazing IRL? Yes."

More fans agreed that Khloe edited the photos to look unlike herself, as someone else wrote: "She doesn't look like this though."

"She is gorgeous - it’s just not her real face," an additional person said.

'ARE YOU OKAY?'

Khloe's drastic body transformation has prompted some fans to reach out to show their support for the E! alum.

Following her weekend in paradise, Kim, 41, shared photos of herself donning a skimpy bikini on the beach with the caption: “Miami, I miss u already!!”

While many of the model’s fans rushed to the comments section to praise Kim as “beautiful” and “hot,” Khloe commented with a seemingly jealous remark, writing: “B***h.”

Shortly after Khloe made the comment, fans asked if the Good American founder is doing "okay."

One person asked: "Good morning, you ok? I think you're so fit."

While several others left similar messages for the Keeping Up With the Kardashians alum.

LOVE CHILD SCANDAL

Aside from her appearance, the Hulu star has been dealing with the aftermath of her baby daddy Tristan Thompson's love child scandal.

The former couple - who share three-year-old daughter True - split after he confirmed in January that he fathered a child with fitness trainer Maralee Nichols.

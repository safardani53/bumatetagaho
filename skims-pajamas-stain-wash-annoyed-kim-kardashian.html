I bought $110 Skims pajamas and they have the grossest looking stain after a wash, Im mad annoyed

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, May 19, 2024

A TRENDY shopper has been left speechless after spotting a glaring problem with her Skims pajamas. 

She was excited to put on the fashionable nightwear before going to bed when she suddenly noticed something was wrong.

Adi Malnick (@adimalnick) has garnered over 145,000 followers on TikTok, where she shares her latest beauty finds.

The New York-based makeup artist took to the social media platform to ask for advice on a major issue with her Skims pajamas.

“I was getting ready to go shower and put my Skims pajamas on until I noticed this was my first time washing it and like,” Adi said.

She held up the stylish gray pajama top to reveal a huge dark stain under the arms which she was unsure was caused by what.

She frowned and opened her mouth in shock as she showed that the marks were under both arms. 

“Excuse me Ms Kimberly what in the world is that,” she said as if speaking to Skims founder Kim Kardashian directly.

“I don’t sweat when I put this on so I don’t know what that is.

“I use the Salt and Stone deodorant, so what is this?

“This should not be happening to a $110 pajama. I’m annoyed, I am really annoyed like what is this?

“Has this happened to anyone else? Because this is not OK. Do I reach out to customer service?”

Adi captioned the post: “I’m mad annoyed and disappointed M. Kimberly! @Skims @KimKardashian.”

The video quickly racked up over 5,600 views and hundreds of likes from people who were grateful that she shared her experience.

Commenters spoke candidly about their own Skims purchases as many admitted they’ve also had issues. 

“I honestly feel like it’s such hit or miss. I have pieces I love that wear well and some that the quality isn’t there. I feel like they keep adding,” one person commented.

“It’s s***ty af [as f**k] I don’t know why people keep buying it,” another wrote.

“My sister had issues with the Skims bodysuit.

"It was great her first try on then after washing the shape was completely off and very uncomfortable,” a third said.

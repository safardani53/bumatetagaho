Zayn Malik Wears Gigi Hadid's Glasses, Fans Freak Out

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, April 12, 2024

Seeing double! Fans couldn't believe their eyes earlier this week when Zayn Malik shared a selfie via Instagram of him wearing what appeared to be Gigi Hadid's glasses!

The hot new couple, who Us Weekly exclusively revealed were dating on Monday, Nov. 23, have been spending a lot of time together recently.

And on Tuesday, Nov. 24, Malik shared a photograph of himself via Twitter which showed off not only his new gray hair color, but also a pair of oh-so-familiar glasses!

pic.twitter.com/xE7sdcw3ww

As Us reported previously, later that evening, the loved-up duo enjoyed a low-key date night at West Hollywood hotspot The Nice Guy, where they were joined by the model's younger sister, Bella Hadid.

And what was Gigi, 20, wearing? None other than the identical pair of glasses Malik, 22, had sported earlier in the day.

"Zayn and gigi already sharing glasses. OMG relationship goals," one excited Twitter user gushed.

"Zayn and gigi shared glasses…" chimed in another. "I can't believe they're married with 3 children and 2 dogs!"

There's no denying the communal glasses could be a sign that things are getting serious between the pair. A source told Us the pair were "inseparable" inside the Hollywood hotspot on Tuesday, and sat in a booth, holding hands, and whispering to each other.

"They seem equally super into each other, texting each other all the time,” another source previously told Us of the happy pair. “They’ve only been hanging out a couple weeks."

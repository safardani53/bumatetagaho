Nina Agdal, Lily Aldridge, and Chrissy Teigen!

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, September 16, 2024

Kate Upton has been dethroned, but at least it took three supermodels to do it. Nina Agdal, Lily Aldridge, and Chrissy Teigen cover the 2014 Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Issue!

The stunning threesome appears topless and nearly bottomless, facing away from readers but looking back toward the camera and flashing million-dollar smiles.

Not sure if this can combat the polar vortex, but the SI Swimsuit Issue is always a nice February surprise in your mailbox, or inbox nowadays. Feast your eyes below:

The buzzed-about 50th anniversary cover was revealed Thursday night on Sports Illustrated‘s Swim Daily blog, before getting its TV debut on Jimmy Kimmel Live.

Chrissy Teigen, who is married to R&B singer John Legend, has been Swimsuit Issue (and social media) staple in 2010, but this is the beauty’s first cover.

It’s the rookie year for Lily Aldridge, a Victoria’s Secret model married to Kings of Leon member Caleb Followill, while Nina Agdal made her SI debut in 2012.

As for that Barbie Swimsuit issue cover, that was clever advertising by Mattel and SI, though it is said to wrapped around at least 1,000 copies of the magazine.

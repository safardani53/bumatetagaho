Walmart is selling a $2,275 barn-style tiny home that features skylights and arrives within two week

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 18, 2024

WALMART is selling an impressive barn-style tiny home, which features skylights and arrives in only two weeks.

The retail giant has priced the home at $2,275.

There is also an option to finance the structure at $116 per month.

The product, which is marketed as an outdoor storage shed, has received a 4.1-star rating on the retailer's website.

It has a number of impressive features, including four skylights, and arrives as quickly as two weeks.

"We love it! Directions along with the build app made this a pretty straightforward assembly," said one shopper, who gave it a five-star review.

"We had a concrete base poured and a build party to erect it. It's spacious, sturdy, and cute. We added mission sensor lights to the inside," they continued.

Not everyone was as pleased though.

"Had high hopes for this shed. Loved it through the summer and fall. Then one snowstorm left enough snow on the roof that the whole thing collapsed with almost none of the pieces salvageable," one shopper wrote.

"If you live anywhere with snow do not buy," they warned.

Many people have converted outdoor sheds into stylish and liveable tiny homes in recent years.

One woman shared her journey on TikTok.

Sarah (@simplysarah1908) said that she decided to make the leap and convert an outdoor shed into a tiny home so she could be debt free by age 25.

"Refused to pay rent the rest of my life so I decided to convert a shed into a tiny home," she explained in one clip.

Tiny homes have become a popular alternative to the classic family home or apartment, thanks to their affordability.

They are even being sold on Amazon.

The company's miniature home retails for just $3,000.

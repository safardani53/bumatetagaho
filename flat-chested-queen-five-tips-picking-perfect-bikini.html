I'm a flat-chested queen who lives near the beach - my five top tips to picking the perfect bikini a

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

A FLAT-CHESTED queen has revealed her top five tips to finding the most flattering bikini for summer.

Harmonie Vachon, who lives by the beach in the US, revealed that most bikinis aren't made to flatter small chests.

However, she revealed the best styles to go for to accentuate your boobs - and the ones to totally avoid.

In a YouTube video, she said: "If you want a little boost of confidence at the beach and you want to feel proportionate and not like a 12-year-old boy like myself, these tips will be helpful for you."

The first bikini, Harmonie revealed you should pick up was the banded style.

This style has a thicker bottom band to help accentuate the boobs and emphasise your waist.

The blonde beauty recommended steering clear of triangle bikini tops.

The fashion fan added: "This triangle shape completely flattens you out, it doesn't really do much for you and your figure."

She also said to stay away from straight-cut bandeau tops.

"If you're flat-chested like myself, the straight-cut bandeau is going to leave you with nothing and no shape," she explained.

Instead, Harmonie opted for a ruffled bandeau top - to give the chest some extra volume to give the illusion of a larger chest.

Next, the beach babe suggested opting for a 'sports top' style bikini to balance out your curves.

"Normally, I wouldn't say this is a good cut for smaller chests, but actually, if you look at the ratio of this kind of top, it takes up a lot of surface area which creates the appearance of a bigger bust," she added.

Not only does this style look great, but it's also perfect if you like to do more physical activities at the beach such as volleyball as it'll keep them secure.

Finally, the fashion whizz recommended a crop-fitted bikini top - the band of this will go all the way down to the waist and is perfect for enhancing your curves, or lack of.

The video has since gone viral with over 110k views and people were quick to thank Harmonie for her tips in the comments section.

One person said: "Thank you so much for these, I’ve been struggling with my self confidence but I really love these styles. I plan on getting some of these."

Another commented: "I cannot thank you enough !! this was so helpful."

"Thank you!! I ended up getting a ruffled swimsuit, it’s coming in the post tomorrow. Very helpful video," wrote a third.

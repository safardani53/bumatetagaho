Aldis selling an anti-ageing LED facemask dupe for 250 less than designer brand - and it reduce

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 6, 2024

SHOPPERS are rushing to Aldi to nab their latest selection of beauty dupes, including a product similar to a £300 LED facemask for £250 cheaper.

In the video, the TikToker said: "This LED facemask is a dupe of the Currentbody one which costs a whopping £300."

LED facemasks have become one of the hottest new trends in the anti-ageing industry and the lights penetrate deep into your skin cells to stimulate new collagen, which reduces the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

The Currentbody facemask is clinically proven to reduce the appearance of wrinkles in just four weeks.

Indulge in the beauty treatment from the comfort of your own home. You can choose from 3 different wavelengths of light to suit your skin."

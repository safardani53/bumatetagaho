Career

The BBC commissioned Garvey and Elbow to write the theme song for the 2012 London Olympics, which Elbow performed at the Olympics’ closing ceremony. Garvey produced and recorded the I Am Kloot album Natural History in 2001 and his other work. Garvey also made an appearance on Massive Attack’s 2010 album Heligoland.

He is an honorary member of the British Academy of Songwriters, Composers, and Authors (BASCA). In April 2012, Garvey was named a Manchester Craft and Design Centre patron. In recognition of his outstanding contribution to music, he was awarded an honorary doctorate from Manchester Metropolitan University in July of the same year.

Guy Garvey has been a presenter on BBC 6 Music on Sunday afternoons from 2 p.m. to 4 p.m., British time, since 2007. Garvey hosted Music Box, an iPlayer-exclusive series that featured emerging and established bands throughout 20 episodes. He previously hosted a show on XFM on Sunday evenings.

Garvey announced in 2015 that he would be releasing his first solo studio album while continuing to serve as Elbow’s lead songwriter. In 2015, Garvey played Kayleigh’s brother-in-law Steve in the BBC television sitcom Peter Kay’s Car Share.

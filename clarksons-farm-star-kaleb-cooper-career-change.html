Clarkson's Farm star Kaleb Cooper reveals big career change away from hit show

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, March 18, 2024

KALEB Cooper has announced an exciting new career change after walking away from Clarkson's Farm.

The 24 year old rose to fame thanks to his appearances on Jeremy Clarkson's hit Amazon Prime series but he has now left the rural Cotswolds life behind for something even bigger and better.

Kaleb was Jeremy's right-hand man on the programme but has now decided to step out on his own.

He will now be treading the boards in theatre after confirming he will be heading out on the road with his very own theatre show.

It comes after he teased an exciting announcement to his fans on Instagram last week and now all has been revealed.

In a post last week, he admitted he was "building up the courage to do something very special".

More on Kaleb Cooper

Confirming his first solo tour, he wrote: "I’m braving it and leaving Chippy for a UK theatre tour!

"I’ll be announcing my first show tomorrow morning, that’s going on sale from 10am."

He later confirmed that his first stop would be Nottingham as he joked that the theatre was a "big old barn".

Kaleb said: "The first available date to book tickets for my national tour is going to be in a big old barn called the Nottingham Royal Concert Hall!"

At the start of the year, he revealed he and fiancée Taya are having a baby girl.

The happy couple, who got engaged on Christmas Day, found out the sex when pink smoke bellowed out of two tractors.

Kaleb, 24, and Taya, 24, were delighted and marked the moment with a big hug.

Their little girl is due in June and follows their first child Oscar, who was born last March.

John Rhode Wins Biggest Loser: Battle of the Ages, PHOTOS, VIDEO

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, April 15, 2024

The season finale of The Biggest Loser: Battle of the Ages aired Tuesday night on NBC. The fifteen competitors returned for one final weigh-in and an opportunity to vie for two mega-prizes.

Competing for $250,000 were Ramon Medeiros, Antone Davis and John Rhode.

The winner of the grand prize and the title is John Rhode, 40, of Mesa, AZ. He started out at 445, and his current weight is 225 for a total loss of 220. His total percentage was 49.44 which beat out the other two competitors. Antone placed second with 45.19 percent and Ramon third with 43.38 percent.

The secondary prize of $100,000 was up for grabs by the eliminated contestants: Debbie Lounds, Johnny Forger, Patrick Ferrari, Mike Danley, Courtney Rainville, Jennifer Rumple, Jessica Limpert, Joe Mitchell, Bonnie Griffin, Sunny Sinclair, Vinny Hickerson, Becky Comet.

Only three contestants with the highest percentage of weight loss got to step on the scale during the live broadcast. They were Patrick, Vinny and Jennifer.

The $100K winner was Jennifer Rumple, 39, of Alameda, California. Her starting weight was 330. She weighed in at 185 for a total weight loss of 145 lbs., for a loss percentage of 43.94.

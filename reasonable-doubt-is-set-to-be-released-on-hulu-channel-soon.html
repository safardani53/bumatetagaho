Reasonable Doubt Is Set To Be Released On Hulu Channel Soon

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, February 13, 2024

Reasonable Doubt, Hulu’s latest legal drama series, is set to premiere on the site on Tuesday, September 27, 2022, at 3 a.m. ET (tentative time). The show is centered on a lawyer called Jax Stewart, who is a very skilled and efficient defense attorney with a rather warped perspective of the law. The show stars Emayatzy Corinealdi as Jax Stewart, with several others playing crucial supporting parts. Jax, a former public defender, is now a well-known criminal defense attorney in Los Angeles. Continue reading to learn about the narrative of Reasonable Doubt, what to anticipate, and other intriguing facts about the courtroom drama.

What You Should Know About Reasonable Doubt

Hulu released the official trailer for the program on September 8, 2022, providing a glimpse inside Jax’s humorously crazy and interesting existence, complete with cases and convoluted loves. The film begins with Jax’s voiceover stating,

“I keep thinking of Nelson Mandela’s quote: I don’t lose; I either win or learn.” But what if your education comes at the cost of someone spending the rest of their life in prison?”

Overall, the trailer has a humorous tone, as the series blends aspects of comedy and drama, as well as the standard legal drama cliches, which might make for an entertaining watching experience. Similar to The Good Wife, The Good Fight, and Anatomy of a Scandal, viewers can anticipate a snappy, witty, and dramatic series full of twists and turns. According to Hulu, the series’ official summary is as follows:

“In “Reasonable Doubt,” you’ll condemn Jax Stewart for her dubious ethics and outlandish legal interpretations…

till you’re the one in hot water. Then you’ll recognize her for what she is: the most intelligent and courageous defense attorney in Los Angeles, who defies the judicial system at every opportunity.” The sitcom is produced by ABC Signature for Onyx Collective, with co-executive producers Jon Leshay and Shawn Holley. Holley is the famous lawyer that inspired Jax’s persona.

A short glance at the cast of Reasonable Doubt

Emayatzy Corinealdi plays protagonist Jax Stewart in the courtroom drama. Corinealdi looks gorgeous in the clip, perfectly expressing her character’s turbulence, lunacy, uncertainty, and despair. EP Series Kerry Washington calls Corinealdi a “star.” It will be fascinating to observe how her character develops during the series. Corinealdi has been in a number of notable films and television episodes, including The Red Line, Hand of God, and Middle of Nowhere, to mention a few.

Michael Ealy and Sean Patrick Thomas play crucial supporting roles with Corinealdi. Michael Ealy is best known for his roles in programs such as The Good Wife, Bel-Air, and Californication. In the clip, Ealy looks great, and his character is supposed to play an important part in Stewart’s life.

Sean Patrick Thomas is most recognized for his roles in Save the Last Dance, in which he co-starred with Kerry Washington, as well as in the Barbershop movie, Vixen, and The District. The series is directed by Raamla Mohamed, who also acts as an executive producer, while the premiere episode is directed by Kerry Washington. On Tuesday, September 27, 2022, don’t miss Reasonable Doubt on Hulu.

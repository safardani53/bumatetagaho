Aunjanue Ellis' Short Bio

Aunjanue L. Ellis was born on 21st February 1969 in San Francisco, California, United States of America. She is an American movie, stage, and television actress and a producer as well. She began her career in acting in theater.

She grew up along with her grandmother in McComb, Mississippi. She attended Tougaloo College and completed her Bachelors of Arts in African-American studies from Brown University. She studied acting at the New York University’s Tisch School of Arts.

She first started her career from the theater and debuted from the movie Girls Town. She has also made her appearances in many television series. Ellis has also been featuring in the TV series Quantico as Miranda Shaw.

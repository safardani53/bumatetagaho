Catherine Jane Ford, known by her stage name Catherine Tate, is an English actress, comedian and writer. She has won numerous awards for her work in the BBC's The Catherine Tate Show sketch series, as well as was nominated for the International Emmy Awards and seven BAFTA awards. Tate played Donna Noble on the 2006 special Doctor Who Christmas and then reprogrammed her role, becoming the tenth Doctor's regular companion in Series Four in 2008. Following the success of The Catherine Tate Show, Tate plays Joannie Taylor in the BBC One comedy series by Catherine Tate's Nan. In 2011, she started playing recurring Nellie Bertram in the US version of The Office and played a regular role until the end of the series. Tate plays Sarah Postern on the BBC One sitcom Big School. Since 2017, she has been playing the voice of Magica De Spell in the animated series DuckTales.

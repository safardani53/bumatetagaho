Five items from Aldi starting at $3.89 - and customers are excited over the 'sampler' return

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, June 19, 2024

ALDI summertime items have returned – with shoppers already racing to stores to try five of them.

With each season comes new additions to each grocery store’s lineup, and the discount retailer is no different.

The company regularly updates its Aldi Finds page for new deals and discounts on newly released items waiting to be discovered in stores.

Here are the top five items customers should look out for this summer at their local store.

1. LIVEGFREE GLUTEN FREE CHEESECAKE SAMPLER

Shoppers are already rushing to taste test a new dessert that is designed to satisfy those with a sweet tooth, no matter their diet restrictions.

The new liveGfree Cheesecake Sampler is entirely gluten-free, so it could be a good option for everyone you invite to your Labor Day or Fourth of July party.

Read more about Aldi

The gluten-free “supreme sampler” comes with two slices of chocolate chip, lemon swirl, New York style, and strawberry swirl cheesecakes.

Each assortment costs only $9.99.

One ecstatic shopper shared their excitement on Facebook: “OMG! This cheesecake is fabulous!”

2. UTILITY TOTE

Your trip to the pool likely needs an upgrade, and that’s why Aldi has stocked up on its pool bag totes.

Most read in Money

Coming in a variety of designs, the crane foldable utility tote is available for just $9.99.

Shoppers can choose whether they prefer the avocado or honeybee look or if they’d rather choose a floral or fruit bag style.

The bag is entirely collapsible for simple storage and has long handles so you can easily tote around your pool towel and any snacks or supplies you might need.

3. ANIMAL FLOATS

Another way to elevate your next pool party? Stock up on Aldi’s massive collection of animal floats and pool noodles.

The creative floatation devices come in unicorn, penguin, and shark varieties for $29.99 each.

You can also purchase more traditional floating seats or noodles for the same price.

Aldi has always made sure to have a variety of aesthetically pleasing pool supplies on hand for the summer months.

Two years ago, it debuted as a donut float for your pool party adventures.

“If you have any food Instagram-obsessed pool guests this summer, their faces will go full-on Heart Eyes Emoji when they see a giant inflatable doughnut floating in the water,” a reviewer wrote in Taste of Home.

4. PATRIOTIC MINI CUPCAKES

After all that summer swimming, Aldi has you covered on the snack front its Delish! Patriotic Mini Cupcakes.

Priced at just $3.89 for 12 cupcakes, this treat is a simple and easy dessert to bring to any Fourth of July party you might be invited to.

Each box comes with half the cupcakes with blue and white frosting, plus some festive sprinkles to top the dessert off.

5. SUMMER GELATO

If you’re looking for a cooler dessert during the summer months, the store is offering Sundae Shoppe Summer Gelato in assorted flavors for $4.29.

Shoppers are regularly surprised when they find out just how good Aldi’s discount frozen treats and ice cream can be.

This year’s flavors include Birthday Cake, Mocha Cookie Crumble, and Peach Cobbler.

So no matter where your taste buds lie, you’ll likely find the perfect answer to your craving.

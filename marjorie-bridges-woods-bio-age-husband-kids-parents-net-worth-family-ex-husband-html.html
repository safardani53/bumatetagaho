Marjorie Bridges-Woods Bio: Husband &amp; Family At Age, 53! Net Worth, Ex Husband

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

Even after being married twice in their life with different individuals, Marjorie and her husband Steve are now strappingly leading a perfect family life as a strong couple.

Marjorie Bridges-Woods is an American socialite, fashion icon, and internet personality, who rose to fame after getting married to the acclaimed television host and comedian Steve Harvey in 2007.

SteveTVshow: Marjorie Harvey discusses how she met Steve Harvey and their early relationship days (Published on 6 October 2018)

Starting from a fashion blog The Lady Loves Couture, Marjorie has built a vast empire from her various business ventures.

Marjorie Bridges-Woods Bio

The fashion icon Marjorie was born on 10 October 1964 in the United States. The fashion icon is a stepmother to twin daughters Karli and Brandi, and son Broderick Steve Jr and Wynton after she married to Steve Harvey.

She joined the ‘University of Memphis’ but dropped out of college due to her bad report on studies.

Marjorie Bridges-Woods Relationship: Marries Steve Harvey aged 53

Marriage is not just about tieing the knots but it is about understanding one another and it never regards age.

Marjorie tied the wedding knot with her longtime on-off boyfriend, Steve Harvey, a renowned radio and TV show host, in 2007. According to Essence, they met at a Memphis comedy club in 1990, where Steve stood on stage and humorously told her, “I don’t know who you are, but I’m going to marry you.”

The couple dated briefly before splitting. Marjorie, in a 2010's interview with Essence, also revealed that they were friends’ years ago and dated for some time.

She also admitted that none of them broke their heart; it was just the long-distance relationship that made them go their separate ways. Steve further stated to People that his bodyguard was the one who told him to date, Marjorie.

Husband-Wife: Marjorie with her husband, Steve Harvey (Photo: Marjorie Harvey’s Instagram)

The power couple Marjorie and Steve share seven kids together. She has three children: Morgan, Jason, and Lori from her previous marriages. Meanwhile, her husband Steve also has four children, twin daughters Brandi and Karli, and two sons, Broderick and Wynton.

Marjorie and Steve’s Children at the Hilton Chicago on 3 May 2014 (Photo: Heavy.com)

Although they share the children from their past relationships, the couple raises the children as real siblings. Marjorie, in an interview with People in 2016, spoke about her blended family. She said;

“We knew this was right. But I just told them, ‘Everyone is included. Everyone has access to their parents. Whatever you didn’t have before, don’t let that interfere with what you can have now.’ ”

Even though they do not have children of their own, they are leading a happy family life together.

Recently, on February 2018 on the Valentine’s Day, Marjorie wished her husband Steve a Happy Valentine’s day, saying that he makes her heart sing every day.

Marjorie and her Spouse’s Past Relationships

Marjorie, like her current husband Steve, was married twice in her life. As per the sources, she was married to two notorious drug dealers. Her first husband was the drug lord, Jim L. Townsend, who was sentenced to life in prison and served 26 years before President Barack Obama gave him an eleventh-hour pardon in January 2017.

She shared her first two children Jason and Morgan with Jim.

Marjorie split with Jim less than five years after his sentence, and Having divorced him, it seemed as if she wanted to run away from the antisocial life but her addiction to it couldn't let her run away all that soon.

For the second time, she got married to another drug seller Donell Woods, similar to her ex-husband. As expected, Donell was soon caught by Police due to his anti-social activity which resulted in a divorce in 2001.

She had her third child, daughter Lori with her second husband. She got and as of now, has no relationship with her ex-husbands.

Similarly, on the other side of the moon, her husband Steve was also married twice before marrying Marjorie. He was wedded to his first wife, Marcia Harvey, from 1980 to 1994. He shares three children with Marcia, twin daughters Karli and Brandi and son Broderick.

After divorce with Marcia, he got married to his second wife, Mary Shackelford, in 1996. It was exactly when Stevie came to know about Marjorie Harvey and was often seen following spending time with her.

It was obvious that relationship insecurities issues would rise among him and his wife Shackelford and it happened too which resulted in a divorce in 2005.

Sneak-Peak On Her Professional Career

Even though Marjorie is the wife of a man whose net worth is around $140 million and annual salary about $40 million, she is not the one to sit back home and let his man do all the job. She is a businesswoman on her own and runs her own fashion blog.

With her husband support and encouragement, in 2014, she turned her passion into a fashion and lifestyle blog, ‘The Lady Loves Couture’ which explores her exciting fashion excursions while also providing exclusive insight and tips for living lavishly on any budget.

She also runs the Steve and Marjorie Harvey Foundation (SMHF) which officially inaugurated in 2010 for reaching out the unreached.

Natalia Altea Jiménez Sarmento, known as Natalia Jiménez, is a Spanish singer and songwriter. She is considered one of the most important Spanish singers. She has received Grammy and Latin Grammy Awards and has sold more than 3 million albums worldwide during her solo career. She has recorded duets with major Latin music stars, including Marc Anthony, Daddy Yankee and Ricky Martin among others.

Popular fast food chain adds two new menu items starting at $3 - but theres a catch

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 12, 2024

JACK In The Box has announced two new menu items, but they aren't available to everyone just yet.

The fast food joint is slowly rolling out new Crispy Chicken Jack Wraps at select locations.

For just $3, diners can grab either the classic or spicy wrap.

Both wraps feature a crispy chicken strip, shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, pickles, Good Good sauce, and buttermilk ranch dressing wrapped up in a warm tortilla.

The difference is that the spicy version features jalapenos and spicy Good Good sauce.

Diners should hold their excitement as the new additions won't be available nationwide until June.

At the moment, select Jack In The Box locations in San Diego, California, are already serving the wraps to gauge customer feedback before they're released everywhere, Chew Boom reported.

MORE JACK IN THE BOX NEW ITEMS

This is just one of many menu innovations Jack In The Box has put out recently.

Both the All American Ribeye Steakhouse Burger and the Bacon All American Ribeye Steakhouse Burger joined the menu this week.

Each one comes with a 100 percent ribeye beef patty, natural cheddar cheese, aioli, red onion, lettuce, and tomato on a potato bun.

"Steakhouse quality but make it Jack," the burger joint put in its menu description.

The All American is priced at $6.89 while its bacon buddy costs $7.29.

Fans rejoiced after Jack In The Box announced it was bringing back the popcorn chicken.

While it was initially introduced in 2020, it was short-lived.

“POPCORN CHICKEN IS BACK, STOP MESSAGING ME PLS - with peace and love from the social media manager,” the Jack in the Box tweet read.

“Bullying worked,” tweeted a fan of the popcorn chicken.

“Smh y'all would not let down,” Jack in the Box responded.

The company doubled down on its customer appreciation by also bringing back the Sauced and Loaded Fries.

Diners can even get a special spicy version that has spicy ranch and jalapenos.

Kim Kardashian's Favorite Skincare Products are Expensive

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Ariel Tejada/Instagram

By now we've all accepted the fact that being Kim Kardashian is a lot of work. From her two-hour long makeup and hair glam sessions to her hardcore workout routine with celeb trainer Gunnar Peterson, she puts in the effort to make sure she's fully Kim Kardashian at all times.

But she's not stingy with her secrets. This morning, she walked fans through her insanely expensive and thorough morning skincare routine on Snapchat. If you're looking to invest some time (and a lot of money) in order to achieve Kim's sought-after skin, follow her routine below.

WHAT: This Works In Transit No Traces Pads
COST: $31

Kim begins her day by rubbing these rosewater- and mint-infused pads all over her face. On Snapchat, Kim said she loves using any products with rose, so clearly, this fits the bill.

Kim Kardashian

WHAT: Anastasia Beverly Hills Hydrating Oil
COST: $40

Today, Kim decided to moisturize her skin with this jojoba, borage, black currant and meadowfoam seed oil. An extra tip from Kim for optimal hydration: "I kind of put this all over my body too," she said.

Kim Kardashian

WHAT: Guerlain Orchidée Impériale The Longevity Concentrate
COST: $510

Next, Kim moves on to this pricey Guerlain serum, because she loves the scent of the brand's products. "I'm going to put this on all over my face after the oil, but wait 10 minutes for it all to soak in," she shares.

Kim Kardashian

WHAT: Lancer Contour Décolleté
COST: $185

Obviously, the woman who started the whole contouring craze can't go a day without incorporating it somewhere in her beauty routine. Today she takes a different approach than just chiseling out her cheeks: she uses this firming concentrate from Lancer to give some firm,defined shape on her neck and décolleté.

Kim Kardashian

WHAT: Kiehl's Super Multi-Corrective Eye-Opening Serum
COST: $48

Under-eye bags and wrinkles get the best of us — even Kim K. She finishes off her skin with this five-in-one eye cream that lifts, firms, smoothes, restores and hydrates. "I've never used this eye serum before, but I've got to start wearing more eye cream," Kim admits.

Kim Kardashian

TOTAL COST: $814

Bonus step! If that's not enough, throw in some body oil and lotion before heading out to start your day. On a day-to-day basis, Kim rotates between her three faves: Blue Lagoon Iceland Silica Softening Bath & Body Oil, Elizabeth Arden's Eight Hour Cream All-Over Miracle Body Oil and Noodle & Boo's Elasticity Oil.

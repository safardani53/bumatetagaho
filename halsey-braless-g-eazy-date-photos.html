Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, March 31, 2024

Halsey, 23, doesn’t ever shy away from taking fashion risks, and that includes ones that reveal her nipples. The “Bad At Love” singer rocked a casual look while headed to lunch with G-Eazy, 28, in Beverly Hills on Dec. 28. Her underwear peeked out the top of a pair of grey sweatpants, which she paired with a pale pink tee that she knotted in the front. The look was effortlessly cool (like her), while still managing to be pretty daring (again, like her) since you could see her braless nipples through her top. Meanwhile, her boyfriend also rocked a t-shirt, but a graphic one, which he paired with acid-wash jeans and a baseball cap. See their outfits below!

The “Now Or Never” singer’s braless outfit wasn’t the only interesting thing about her look. She also debuted a new platinum blonde hairstyle! Halsey’s constantly switching up her hair — she’s dyed it an array of different pastel hues, worn it at various lengths, and changed the styling of it multiple times. Her long blue and yellow hair became an iconic staple of her Badlands era. Most recently, she had been rocking long dark braids, which you can see in one of her Instagram posts from Christmas. In the photo, she can be seen hugging G-Eazy while sporting cornrows in her hair. See more pictures of the couple here!

If you can’t get enough of this couple though, you’re in luck. The pair partnered up for their newest track “Him & I” which came with an incredibly romantic music video. So while you look at these adorable pics of their outing, you can also listen to their love song on repeat. Hopefully we’ll have even more outings and musical melodies to look forward to from these two in 2018!

Emma Watson and Chris Pratt look unrecognisable as stars share their old headshots - but Sofia Verga

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 28, 2024

EMMA Watson and Chris Pratt look unrecognisable in their old headshots.

The stars were amongst those who joined the craze of sharing their first promo shots, revealing their transformation over the years.

But while Emma and Chris might have completely overhauled their looks, Modern Family actress Sofia Vergara looks exactly the same.

The star, now 45, delighted fans sharing a model shot of herself taken back in 1992 in Bogota.

This Morning presenter Holly Willoughby also got people talking earlier this week as she revealed her natural hair colour.

Speaking on the morning TV show, Holly said: "Over the weekend you might have seen the hashtag 'old headshots' trending with many Hollywood stars sharing snaps of themselves before they were famous.

"So this prompted us to dig through our old snaps and find some early shots of the whole This Morning team."

They then showed co-host Phillip Schofield sporting a head of dark hair and Holly went: "Aww, little face."

Holly's face then popped up and she quipped: "Slightly closer to my natural hair colour there, I was a brunette once upon a time."

Holly laughed as she saw her head shot appear on screen

They then shared an old snap of their This Morning boss posing next to a plane.

Holly then hinted they were in trouble for sharing the picture as she said: "I can’t repeat what’s just been said in my ear. We've probably been fired."

Phillip then said: “Should we finish it or should we just go now?”

Viewers were also treated to snaps of Dr Chris, Alison Hammond, Alice Beer and John Torode.

Earlier today, the Good Morning Britain team showed off their headshots with Charlotte Hawkins looking like Marilyn Monroe.

Piers Morgan, 53, was just 19-years-old in his snap from his early reporting days, co-host Susanna Reid looked no different in hers and said it was because she couldn’t find her earliest one, while Charlotte looked like a 50s film star.

“She looks like Marilyn Monroe! Oh my days!” said a gobsmacked Susanna, as Charlotte mocked the rather unnatural pose she was pulling.

While Piers told Richard he should have been in Take That with his shot from 1994. And of course, Susanna couldn’t resist making a cosmetic surgery joke about Piers.

She asked him: “Is this the picture you took to the surgeon in Los Angeles when you were there last week? Did you say, ‘Make my forehead look like that again’?

Piers quipped: “Once again, as you can see no lines then, no lines today, it’s good living.”

Susanna's teasing comes as the controversial host hasn’t been able to shake off accusations that he’s had work done following some time off the show.

On today's Loose Women Andrea McLean shocked fans as she shared her old headshot.

The 48-year-old joined fellow panellists Kelly Brook, Coleen Nolan and Jane Moore in showing off their first professional promo snaps.

But rather than just having a picture of her head, Andrea's shot, taken when she was around 27-years-old, sees her wearing a pair of low slung jeans with a cropped cardigan.

The sexy top was tied with just a simple bit of cord, revealing she wasn’t wearing a bra beneath.

Meanwhile Kelly Brook’s old headshot was also had fans talking as the star, now 38, looks almost unrecognisable.

In the picture, which she says was taken when she was around 18, Kelly is wearing a pink crop top while her hair is styled with a fringe.

Jane Moore’s headshot sees her looking like the spitting image of 80s power ballad queen Bonnie Tyler with big hair.

She said it was taken when she was just 23 and editing The Sun’s Bizarre column.

Who Is Keleigh Sperry? All About Miles Tellers Wife Hollywood Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 6, 2024

Miles Teller has been establishing himself as one of the biggest names in Hollywood for over a decade. After breaking out in The Spectacular Now, the actor, 36, got even more famous for his role in the Divergent film series, but he got some of the most critical acclaim when he starred in the 2014 film Whiplash. Since then, he’s gone on to other major movies and franchises, like the hit Top Gun: Maverick.

In his personal life, Miles has enjoyed just as much success with his wife Keleigh Sperry by his side since 2019. The pair even got to show their love when they both starred in Taylor Swift’s 2021 music video for “I Bet You Think About Me.” In fact, Keleigh has been close friends with Taylor for a while, and she even joined the “Cruel Summer” singer at the 81st Golden Globes in January 2024. The model also revealed that she gave Taylor a beautiful ring for her 34th birthday in December 2023.

Keleigh’s friendship with Taylor goes back quite a while. In 2014, she was present at the singer’s birthday party, and even posted a photo of herself with Taylor and Jessica Szohr on Twitter at the time. Their friendship has clearly persevered, as she joined the singer at the Globes. Get to know more about Keleigh here!

What Does Keleigh Sperry Do?

Keleigh is a model. In addition to her modeling career, though, she has also done some work as an actress. So far, Keleigh has only starred in two short films, aside from her role in Taylor’s new music video. Perhaps this will be what catapults her acting career to the next level!?

When Did Miles and Keleigh Meet?

Keleigh and Miles met in May 2013 while attending a party for the Black Keys, of whom they had mutual friends. “Keleigh had caught my eye early in the night, and we talked,” Miles revealed in 2019. “I didn’t think it went well, but after a few more attempts, I got her to dance with me, and a week later, we went on a date. After we met, I knew he was The One.” Keleigh added that she “adored” Miles from the “first moment” that she met him.

How Long Have Miles and Keleigh Been Married?

After four years of dating, Miles proposed to Keleigh in August 2017 while they were on a safari in South Africa. The two got married in Sept. 2019 in Maui, Hawaii. They chose the location after vacationing there several times with Keleigh’s family throughout their relationship. “We fell in love with it together,” Keleigh admitted. “We both felt happiest there and wanted it to be a big part of our lives forever.”

Keleigh Is Friends With Shailene Woodley

In 2021, Keleigh and Miles took a couples’ vacation with former couple Shailene Woodley and Aaron Rodgers and posted all about the getaway on social media. Miles and Shailene have worked together before and remained close, and Keleigh has also struck up a close relationship with the actress. During the trip, Keleigh even posted a video of herself jamming out to none other than her other pal Taylor Swift with Aaron!

Keleigh Has Five Siblings

Keleigh revealed in an October 2015 Instagram post that she is one of six siblings. She posted a photo of her family at a wedding and wrote, “My siblings and our significant others. When there’s 6 kids, it quickly becomes 12 and I love it! Love you guys!”

Shannon Miller Biography

Shannon Miller was born on a Thursday, March 10, 1977 in MO. Her birth name is Shannon Lee Miller and she is currently 46 years old. People born on March 10 fall under the zodiac sign of Pisces. Her zodiac animal is Serpent.

Shannon Lee Miller is a former artistic gymnast from Edmond, Oklahoma. She was the 1993 and 1994 World All-Around Champion, the 1996 Olympics balance beam gold medalist, the 1995 Pan Am Games all-around champion, and a member of the gold medal-winning Magnificent Seven team at the Atlanta Olympics. The winner of a combined total of 16 World Championships and Olympic medals between 1991 and 1996, Miller ranks as the most decorated gymnast, male or female, in U.S. history. Miller was also the most successful American athlete, by medal count, at the 1992 Barcelona Games, winning five altogether. Miller is also the tenth most decorated gymnast of all time, by her individual medal count.

She went to Moscow at age 9 to study gymnastics further.

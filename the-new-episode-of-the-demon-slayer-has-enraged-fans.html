The New Episode Of The Demon Slayer Has Enraged Fans

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 20, 2024

It’s been a long time coming, but the fight between Gyutaro and Daki and the Demon Slayer Corps is finally here. Ufotable has exceeded expectations with its high-quality animation.

The show’s art style is well-known, so viewers were eager to see what the new episode would bring. It’s safe to say that after it was released, they went insane, praising and cursing the creators for leaving them with such a terrifying cliffhanger.

After the latest episode of Demon Slayer, fans took to Twitter to express their excitement.

Tengen’s fight with Gyutaro takes a chilling turn in this episode’s opening moments. Hinatsuru, who is aiding the heroes, is attacked by the latter out of nowhere.

Fans are transported to a beautiful time with Tengen and his three wives together in a flashback as a raging Tengen loses his mind.

Tanjiro saves her, and fans are treated to a battle that looks like it was lit up by actual fireworks after Tanjiro saves her. Possibly the most cinematic sequence in the entire series up to this point was the one between Uzui and Gyutaro.

In this episode, the young trio worked together to defeat Daki while avoiding her Obi attacks, which was probably the best part of the episode. This sequence was made perfect by the animation, which demonstrated how well the three have gotten along in battle by continuously attacking and avoiding hits at the same time.

However, Inosuke is stabbed by Gyutaro when he tries to flee with Daki’s severed head in his hands. Tengen’s severed hand can also be seen holding his weapon.

In Demon Slayer, the conflict between the two sides is finally coming to a head. A new episode has been teased, but fans can’t help but wait impatiently for it to be released.

How long have Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song been together and how many kids do they have?

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

MACAULAY Culkin has mostly kept details about his love life under wraps, but fans are privy to his relationship with Brenda Song.

The pair have been together for more than a decade and became engaged in January 2022.

How long have Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song been together?

Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song have been together since 2017.

But the two met back in 2013 when Culkin dropped by on the set of Song's sitcom, Dads.

“Yeah, she totally brushed me off, completely,” the Home Alone star quipped in an episode of his "Bunny Ears" podcast, to which Song responded, "I was focused."

Four years later, they crossed paths again on the set of Seth's Green movie Changeland and finally hit it off.

Culkin admitted in a 2020 profile on Esquire that, in the beginning, he was "waiting for the other shoe to drop," though it never did.

Meanwhile, Song had nothing but nice things to say about him, sharing, "Truly what makes Mack so special is that he is so unapologetically Mack. He knows who he is, and he's 100 percent okay with that. And that to me is an incredibly sexy quality. He's worked really hard to be the person he is."

They also revealed at the time that they were already trying to have kids.

“We’re figuring it out, making the timing work. Because nothing turns you on more than when your lady comes into the room and says, ‘Honey, I’m ovulating.’”

In January 2022, the couple sparked engagement rumors after Brenda was spotted with a diamond ring on her finger.

Two days later, People confirmed they were officially set to marry.

In Culkin's Walk of Fame ceremony, he made sure to honor his longtime partner.

"You are absolutely everything. You're my champion," he said.

"You're the only person happier for me today than I am. You're not only the best woman I've ever known, you're the best person I've ever known.

"You've given me all my purpose. You've given me family."

How many kids do Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song have?

Macaulay Culkin and Brenda Song share two kids.

The duo became parents to their first child together, son Dakota, on April 5, 2021.

On March 17, 2023, Us Weekly reported the couple welcomed their second child.

He was reportedly born before Christmas of 2022.

In an interview with The Cut, Song shed light on how she and Culkin have approached parenthood.

"My fiancé and I are very hands-on. We don’t have a nanny, but my mom has been here with us since my son was born. He’s 9 months old now."

She also explained that they have set up a system that works for their family.

"I think when you and your partner have kids, especially the first few months, you don’t have the patience to be polite with each other.

"Being communicative when you need help is so important. Instead of having set duties, we just feel each other out. I’ll be putting my son down and my partner is like, “Let me feed the animals and get dinner ready.'"

Has Macaulay Culkin even been married?

In 1998, at the age of 18, Macaulay married actress Rachel Miner.

In the announcement of their engagement, the former lovebirds said that they were "thrilled to have found each other as soul mates at such a tender age."

However, their marriage was short-lived, with the two separating in 2000 and divorcing in 2002.

"It is very amicable and they remain [the] best of friends," their spokesperson said at the time.

The same year their divorce was finalized, Culkin started dating actress Mila Kunis.

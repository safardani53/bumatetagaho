How I Caught My Killer Is Set To Released On Hulu

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

How I Caught My Killer, a real crime docuseries from Hulu, will launch on the streaming site on January 12, 2023, at 12:01 a.m. ET. It’s a true crime docuseries that chronicles real-life homicide cases in which victims who left clues aid in the investigation of their murders.

Unlike previous episodes in the genre, this new Hulu series takes a unique approach to real crime by concentrating on groundbreaking evidence left behind by the victims. Read up on all we know about the next Hulu real crime docuseries before it premieres.

What You Need to Know About “How I Caught My Killer”

How I Caught My Killer is a nine-episode docuseries that focuses on many criminal cases in which victims left vital clues that finally helped detectives get to the bottom of the crimes and discover the perpetrators. In-depth interviews, real-life archives, and filmed re-enactments will be used to recreate these instances in the next true crime series. It will also investigate the inadequacies of the American judicial system, as well as the system’s underlying racism and xenophobia, which impedes justice.

Hulu’s official description for How I Caught My Killer is as follows:

“Whether it’s a tense letter to a friend, a frantic text message detailing a heated argument, a private diary entry describing a harrowing experience, or an ominous social media post revealing a motive for murder… these victims all leave a case-breaking clue that will one day help to solve their crime,” says the author.

Is there a trailer available?

Readers who are interested may watch the trailer for How I Caught My Killer below. Cases are reconstructed in the series in such a manner that each one is a nuanced picture of a human person whose life was cut short by a criminal and how these perpetrators would have eluded punishment if it hadn’t been for the victims themselves who left evidence that aided identify the culprit.

These victim-solved crimes reveal a disturbing reality about the shortcomings of the American judicial system to bring its people to justice. It sheds light on the grim realities of many unseen communities and how they become murder victims year after year. In addition to depicting nine incidents, How I Caught My Killer includes poignant interviews with the victim’s friends and family, forcing viewers to face the agony of losing a loved one to murder. It also demonstrates how behavioral changes and social media might aid in the investigation of homicides.

How to Stream the Hulu Documentary Series

Hulu’s How I Caught My Killer debuts on Thursday, January 12, 2023. The series is only available on the streaming platform and costs $7.99 per month with commercials or $14.99 per month without.

True crime is now a pretty popular genre, with all of the big OTT platforms focused on amassing a good library of docuseries. How I Caught My Killer is the newest Hulu phenomenon, following in the footsteps of The Hatchet Wielding Hitchhiker on Netflix. Watch the new series on Hulu starting this Thursday.

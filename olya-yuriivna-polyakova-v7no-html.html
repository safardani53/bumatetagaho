Chanteuse pop Olya Yuriivna Polyakova est né à Ukraine le 17 Janvier, 1979. Elle a 44 ans aujourd'hui.

Ukrainian singer, TV presenter, and comedian who is known for her songs "Queen of the night,""Number one" and"#Flip flops". She is the host of the television show He Cooks, She Manages on TET TV.

Olya Yuriivna Polyakova was born in the Zodiac sign Capricorne (The Sea-Goat), and 1979 is also the year of Goat (羊) in the Chinese Zodiac.

At 15, she began her singing career at the restaurant Bakhus on Kyivska Street in Vinnytsia. She attended the Vinnytsia School of Culture and Arts. She later attended Kyiv National University of Culture and Arts and Petro Tchaikovsky National Music Academy of Ukraine where she received a degree as an opera singer.

In 2017, she was on season 4 of Tantsi z zirkamy, which is Ukraine's version of Dancing with the Stars. She appeared as a guest host on season 5.

She has been married to Vadym Buriakovskyi since 2004. They have two daughters, Maria and Alice.

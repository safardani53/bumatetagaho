How old is Charlotte Crosby and what is her net worth? The US Sun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 20, 2024

CHARLOTTE Crosby has come a long way since her first foray into reality TV and the early years of Geordie Shore.

In April 2022 the published author and TV star revealed she is expecting her first child.

Who is Charlotte Crosby and when was she on Geordie Shore?

Charlotte, 31, was born in Sunderland on May 17, 1990.

Her mum is Letitia, an interior adviser, and has an Instagram page called crosbys_eco_build, where she shows off her stunning home.

The proud mum regularly posts snaps of herself and husband Gary with her bio reading: "Just loving life - parent to two beautiful kids trying to live my best life with a lovely husband."

Charlotte attended St Anthony's Girls School.

She has a younger brother named Nathaniel.

After achieving good GCSE and A-Level results, Charlotte planned on studying criminology at university.

She worked a short stint  behind a bar named Tonic in Sunderland but she quit her regular job when she was selected to appear in Geordie Shore in 2011.

Charlotte became a popular member of the cast – and worked on the show for a whopping 12 series, before leaving in 2017.

What other TV shows has Charlotte been in?

Charlotte’s biggest television victory has got to be her stint in the Celebrity Big Brother house in 2015.

Even though she shocked housemates by wetting herself during the reality show, she managed to win the Channel 5 competition.

Since then she has appeared on a number of other popular ITV shows, including Britain’s Got More Talent, Xtra Factor and Celebrity Juice.

Charlotte also fronted Just Tattoo Of Us with her boyfriend at the time, Stephen Bear.

From 2018 until 2019, she had her own show called The Charlotte Show.

She appeared in the Aussie version of I'm A Celebrity in 2020.

In 2021 she took part in Celebrity The Circle.

What is Charlotte's net worth?

It's fair to say Charlotte is worth a bob or two.

As of 2022 it is thought her fortune is around the £2million mark.

This is through her TV appearances and Instagram endorsements.

Who is Charlotte Crosby dating and who has she dated?

Charlotte's first big romance was her fiery on-off relationship with Geordie Shore co-star Gaz Beadle.

Their relationship finally came to an end following Charlotte's ectopic pregnancy.

While she was undergoing emergency life-saving surgery, Beadle was filming Ex On The Beach - where he slept with Jemma Lucy.

When Charlotte discovered this, despite wanting to patch things up with him, she ended things for good and then left Geordie Shore shortly after.

She went on to date Stephen Bear in 2017 before moving on to Joshua Ritchie in 2019.

Charlotte's current boyfriend is Jake Ankers, who she met in November 2021 through mutual friends.

In April 2022 the couple revealed they were expecting their first baby together.

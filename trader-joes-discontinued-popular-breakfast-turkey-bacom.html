Trader Joe's confirms it's discontinued popular breakfast item leaving fans gasping in shock

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 26, 2024

TRADER Joe's fans have spoken out over fears the popular grocer has discontinued a beloved breakfast product.

One woman headed to Twitter to share the news that the store will no longer be offering their Peppered Uncured Turkey Bacon.

The perfectly seasoned, smokey turkey bacon offered a healthier option for meat-lovers, at just 40 calories per slice.

Shopper, @drakeloveskayla, expressed her anger in a post from March 16.

"Y’all... Trader Joes discontinued their peppered black turkey bacon," she wrote. "I’m so upset."

Kayla was adamant on finding out exactly what happened to the tasty breakfast side and decided to ask an employee for help.

Read more on Trader Joe's

"When a worker checked their system for it and told me the bad news, I literally had my hand on my chest when I gasped," she explained.

Supposedly, the Trader Joe's employee was also disappointed that the turkey bacon had been taken off the shelves.

"She's like I know," Kayla added.

On the Trader Joe's review website, another person mourned the loss of the so-called 'destination item.'

Most read in Money

"We love this bacon. It was a 'destination item' at Trader Joe’s.

"Trader Joe’s is a good distance from where we live, so won’t be making as many trips. Please consider bringing it back," they pleaded.

Trader Joe's is notorious for making changes to their selection, often discontinuing item's that were fan-favorites.

In February, a customer claimed that the budget-grocer was pulling their Salsa Verde Tortilla Chips and planning to give away free bags to get rid of the surplus stock.

Last year, Trader Joe’s quietly discontinued their cashew butter, a peanut butter alternative that many shoppers suggested was more affordable than other brands.

Fortunately, the supermarket often rotates their past selection for new products that are hard to find elsewhere.

Spring shoppers can look forward to trying some out-of-the-box treats like Lemon Mini Sheet Cake, Vanilla Cardona Goat's Milk Cheese, and Mini Almost Everything Bagel Sandwich Crackers.

While these new items will never replace the delicious turkey bacon, at least there are some new products for Trader Joe's loyalists to look forward to.

The U.S. Sun has reached out to Trader Joe's to confirm the discontinuation of their Uncured Peppered Turkey Bacon.

Who is Karen Yapoort Encarnacion:

Karen Yapoort Encarnacion is a famous TV Show Host. She was born on August 3, 1989 and her birthplace is Santo Domingo, Dominican Republic. Karen is also well known as, Model and TV show host who first became known in 2009 when she was crowned Miss Amber, making her the first Dominican to do so. She is best known for presenting the shows Los Duenos del Circo and Vale por Tres. 

Karen is originated from Dominican Republic. Vale por Tres is produced by Roberto Angel Salcedo Sanz and co-hosted by Hony Estrella.

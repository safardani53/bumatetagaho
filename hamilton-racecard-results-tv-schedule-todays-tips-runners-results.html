Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, February 8, 2024

RACING takes place at Hamilton today and we have you covered with all the latest runners, results, tips and going news.

Get yourself along to the racecourse or watch on tv as racing gets underway this afternoon.

What time does racing start at Hamilton?

The first race is at 1.30pm.

There are seven races on the card and the last race is due off at 4.55pm.

What TV channel is Hamiltonon?

You can watch the racing at Hamilton on Racing UK.

Racing UK is on Sky channel 432, Virgin channel 536, and UPC in Ireland on channel 411.

ITV will show the 2.05 and 2.35.

What is the going at the racecourse?

The going is currently Good to Soft, Soft in Places.

Racecourse information with leading jockeys and trainers with runners today:

  • Right-handed loop-shaped course with a 5f run-in and a stiff uphill finish. Middle distance races start in front of the stands and go reverse way up the straight. 5-6f races run on the straight course.
  • Low draw numbers best.
  • Favourites: (2013-18): 210 have won out of 683 races (30.7%). At the previous corresponding meetings, 17 have won out of 56 races (30.4%).
  • Leading trainers (2013-18): K Dalgleish 65-452, M Johnston 49-232, K Ryan 34-156, R Fahey 30-267, D O’Meara 24-135, J Goldie 18-208, I Jardine 15-100.
  • Leading jockeys (2013-18): J Fanning 57-288, G Lee 37-227, P Mulrennan 27-191, P Makin 22-155, D Tudhope 20-102, C Beasley 20-160, T Eaves 18-247.
  • K Ryan won the 2.05 in 2011 and 2012.
  • Longest traveller: Bear Valley (2.35) trained by C Mann, Upper Lambourn, round trip of 734 miles.

Colleen Lopez Living Happily with her Husband, No Children and No Divorce Rumors

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, April 22, 2024

Marriage is the beautiful bond between two soulmates. Fairy tales do come true for some people. Among them, Colleen Lopez is the one whose life is blessed with a loving husband and two gorgeous children.

An American reporter, Colleen is also a renowned television personality. She is popular for her show HSN (Home Shopping Network). She is married to Carlos for three decades now. Isn't it amazing? They have two sons and their lovely family has stood as an example to the world's inspiration.

Let's hear about Colleen's beautiful married life and family!

The married life of Colleen Lopez with Husband Carlos reached over Three Decades

Hardworking and dedicated Colleen is working on her show HSN for 17 years now. She is mainly known with the name Colleen McCarthy. Her passion for travel and gemstone made her choose the work on HSN. She even studied in the Gemological Institute of American and earned her Accredited Jewelry Professional.

However, she started her career as a reporter and a talk show host in the television show She started off the television commercials with brands named Brown & Crisp.

She then joined KSTP's Good Company as the Bargain Hunter from where she became the popular local tv personality. She then developed her talent and established herself as a world-class shopper and expert TV host.

Love builds on the walls of friendship. Well, Colleen happened to find love in her long-time best friend Carlos so they got married on February 6, 1982, at the St. Paul Cathedral.

It was the same church where Colleen's parents got married. Then they went to Mexico for their honeymoon.

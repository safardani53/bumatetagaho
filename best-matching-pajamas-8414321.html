The 20 Best Matching Pajamas of 2023

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, May 8, 2024

It’s the most wonderful time of the year to get into a festive spirit and lean into holiday traditions. And, one of our favorite ways to lean into the yuletide season is with matching pajamas for couples, siblings, or the entire family (including your pets). 

“Matching pajamas provide a sense of family tradition, bonding, and togetherness for families,” Naina Singla, a fashion stylist and style expert, tells PEOPLE.  “There are very few opportunities for a family to wear the exact same outfits, and this is one easy way to have a good excuse to dress up and coordinate,” she adds, noting that it can be for immediate families as well as extended with cousins and grandparents joining in on the fun. 

With the holidays right around the corner, this is one of the best times of year to buy matching pajamas. But, it’s also one of the busiest times of the year, and there are so many different styles and prints to choose from. To make your holiday shopping a little more efficient, we did the research for you and hand-selected 20 of the best matching pajamas for the holiday season. See our top picks, ahead.

How to Pick the Right Matching Pajamas 

Fabric

When shopping for the best matching pajamas, Cassandra Cannon, the co-founder and chief creative officer at LAKE Pajamas, says to consider fabrics and how you want them to feel when worn. Cannon recommends looking for ultra-soft materials — like modal and pima — and cozy. “Consider what makes you feel most comfortable first and foremost, then look for a print or color that speaks to your holiday style.”

Sizes and Styles

The available sizes and styles are also important. That way, the whole family can participate in the tradition. While some pajama brands carry styles for the entire family — including infants, toddlers, kids, and pets — others might only offer matching pajamas for couples. Similar to fabric, it’s important to also look at the sizes and styles available before getting inspired by the prints.

That being said, if you end up in a situation where you love the holiday prints and pajamas, but there aren’t infant or pet options, you can still achieve coordination. Purchase a onesie or pet bandana in a solid color that matches the patterns in your adult or kid pajama styles. 

Patterns, Prints, and Colors 

Half the fun of Christmas pajamas is choosing a pattern, print, or solid color that makes you and your family feel jolly. Once you consider the fabric, sizes, and styles available, take a look at the different colorways and available prints. Whether you’re a classic plaid or striped family or want to go all-out with a print inspired by your favorite Christmas movie, there are so many different options to choose from. 

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Are matching Christmas pajamas worth it?

    Matching Christmas pajamas are absolutely worth it if you want to create a cherished holiday tradition. While matching pajamas are traditionally worn on Christmas Eve and Christmas morning, Singla says you can get more mileage out of your investment by unveiling them earlier, taking lots of family photos, and even using the matching pajamas to make your holiday card. “You can also stick to one theme year after year, so adults can re-use the pajamas, and younger kids can use older kid’s PJs,” she explains. 

  • When should I buy Christmas pajamas?

    This depends on when you want to unveil them. Some families purchase festive pajamas in November to have enough time to wear them for holiday cards and can get a lot of use out of them throughout the Christmas season. Others prefer to save their holiday PJs for Christmas Eve or even Christmas Day, allowing you more time to purchase your festive jammies. 


    If you’re concerned about time and purchasing online, note the brand’s shipping policy to get an idea of delivery. In cases where you’re a little more pressed for time, Amazon Prime has you covered with two-day shipping on tons of matching Christmas pajamas (including lots on our list, like the Ekouaer Christmas Family Matching Pajamas, Benaive Matching Family Christmas Pajamas, and The Children's Place Family Matching Christmas Pajamas).

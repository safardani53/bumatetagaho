Kiran Rathod Biography

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 16, 2024

Kiran Rathod first came to the Mumbai to finish her graduation and after completing the graduation from Mithibai College of Mumbai, Kiran entered the film industry and seen in her very first Bollywood movie "Yaadein" along with Kareena Kapoor and Hrithik Roshan in 2001.

Initially, Kiran was seen in some Hindi pop songs from which she got a chance to perform for movies. She worked in many Hindi, Tamil, Malayalam, Telugu and Kannada movies. She is a famous South Indian actress working in the Bollywood movie Industry where she gave an impressive performance. She worked in 6 Tamil films in 2003.

Kiran Rathod had an affair with a boy name Rohit when she was in college in 1995. Kiran again was in a relationship with Ramkumar Pachaiyapan, she got married to her husband Ramkumar in 2007.

Kiran Rathod is an Indian nationality actress of Asian ethnicity having height 5 feet 3 inch and weight 58 Kg. Kiran earns her salary from the film industry. She has a net worth of above 50 lakhs rupees.

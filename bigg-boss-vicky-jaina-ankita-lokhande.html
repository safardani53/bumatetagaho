Inside Bigg Boss 17 stars Vicky Jain and Ankita Lokhande's luxury car collection from $150k Porsche

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, May 17, 2024

BIGG Boss 17 stars Vicky Jain and Ankita Lokhande are two of the most famous contestants on the show this season. 

The entrepreneur-celebrity duo is known for living a luxurious lifestyle - and has an impressive car collection worth over $300,000.

Back in 2019, Ankita and Vicky confirmed their relationship before getting married in December 2021. 

They live in an eight-bedroom sea-facing apartment in Mumbai - and are known to lead a lavish life. 

Vicky owns multiple businesses across various sectors - and is reported to have a net worth of more than $1 Billion. 

The couple has made several headlines in the past about their expensive purchases - including an extravagant villa in the Maldives and a private yacht worth $1 Million.

However, not many people know about their obsession with luxury cars. 

Vicky and Ankita together own a fancy car collection - and here are the top cars from their garage.

Porsche 718 Boxster

This sports car is a marvel of driving dynamics - and boasts a turbocharged inline-four engine.

The 718 Boxter is known for its iconic aesthetics, a swift soft top, and a top speed of 170 mph.

Reportedly, Vicky bought the car for $150,000.

Jaguar XF

The Jaguar XF is perhaps one of the most iconic cars in the brand's lineup.

Being a premium saloon, the XF model has a compelling body and a striking interior offering utmost luxury to the passengers.

The car offers a four-wheel-drive terrain, but customers can opt for a rear-wheel-drive model for a thrilling driving experience.

Mercedes-Benz V-Class

Sitting at the epitome of luxury, the rare Mercedes-Benz V-class is the most expensive car owned by Ankita and Vicky.

The multi-utility vehicle is equipped with a robust 2.2-litre straight-four engine, and is paired with a seven-speed automatic transmission system.

The powerhouse of the car produces an impressive 161 BHP, and can reach a top speed of 120mph.

Recently, Ankita Lokhande was reduced to tears after clashing with Vicky inside the Bigg Boss 17 house.

A drama-fuelled promo video showed the couple struggling in the show, with Ankita saying she felt lonely after Vicky's "hurtful" act.

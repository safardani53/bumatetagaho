Kim Kardashian wears huge orange puffer jacket on date night with Kanye West to star-studded NBA gam

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, June 20, 2024

KIM Kardashian donned a burnt-orange puffer jacket next to husband Kanye West at the NBA All-Star game last night.

The superstar showed off her gigantic coat on her date night with hubby Kanye, teaming the look with faux leather trousers and a tight top.

Kim, 39, stayed warm in the duvet-inspired clothing and revealed its size when she stood for The Star Spangled Banner with her 42-year-old spouse, who hid his eyes behind huge shades.

The rapper wore a thin denim jacket and shirt court-side, flanked by J. Cole at the the United Centre in Chicago, Illinois.

Also lining the court was Cardi B, who donned a floor length fur scarf next to Migos’ Quavo and Offset.

They were joined by Spike Lee, Chance The Rapper, and 2 Chainz to watch Team Lebron go head-to-head with Team Giannis.

Kobe Bryant was honored at the game by Jennifer Hudson in a heart-wrenching tribute to the Lakers legend

The Oscar winner, 38, opened the night with a moving performance that honored Kobe, his 13-year-old daughter Gianna and the seven other victims who died in the tragic helicopter crash last month.

She sang the Donny Hathaway classic For All We Know before the game began in front of the NBA's best players as well as a catalogue of famous celebs.

The Grammy-winning singer got chocked up at the beginning of the emotional performance.

Magic Johnson led an eight-second moment of silence to honor Kobe's first Laker jersey number.

The prominent LA sports figure also began the ceremony with a heartfelt speech about Kobe's legacy.

He said: “We will never see another basketball player quite like Kobe.

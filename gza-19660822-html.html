GZA (Rapper) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 16, 2024

Gary Earl Grice, known by his stage names GZA and The Genius, is an American rapper and musician. As a founding member of the hip hop group Wu-Tang Clan, GZA is the "spiritual leader" of the group, both the first member of the group to receive a recording contract and the oldest member. He has appeared in solo projects of his other Clan members and maintained a successful solo career starting with Liquid Swords. His lyrical style often rejects typical rap plotlines in favor of science and various philosophies and is characterized as "equipped with sharp metaphors and smooth flow." An analysis of GZA's lyrics revealed that he has the fourth largest vocabulary in popular hip hop music. He collaborates with an educational group to promote science education in New York City through hip hop.

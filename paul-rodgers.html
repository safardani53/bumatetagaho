Paul Rodgers Biography

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Tall and handsome, Paul Rodgers is a talented singer, songwriter, and musician from the United Kingdom. He has also established himself as a multi-instrumentalist. He is mainly popular in the world for being the vocalist of Free and Bad Company.

George Lucas Age, Biography

George Lucas was born on May 14, 1944, in Modesto, California, United States. He is of mixed ancestry, with English, Scottish, Dutch, French, and Swiss-German ancestors on his father’s side and German and Swiss-German ancestors on his mother’s. In terms of faith, he was raised in a Methodist family.

George’s current age is 79, which he celebrated recently in 2023, and his zodiac sign is Taurus, according to his birthdate. As a result, he is white. His mother, Dorothy Ellinore Lucas, and father, George Walton Lucas Sr., raised him. Wendy Lucas, Ann Lucas, and Kathleen Lucas were his siblings.

In terms of his education, Lucas attended Thomas Downey High School and graduated from Modesto’s Roosevelt Junior High School in 1958. Later, he graduated from the University of Southern California (USC) School of Cinematic Arts in 1962.

George Lucas Height & Weight

George Lucas has an optimum height of 5 ft 7 in (170 cm) and a healthy body weight of 77 kg (169.5 lbs). His hair is gray, and his eyes are dark brown. Furthermore, his body type is average.

George Lucas’s Wife

George Lucas is a married guy, having married his lovely wife Mellody Hobson on June 22, 2013, at Lucas’ Skywalker Ranch in Marin County, California. Mellody joined Ariel Investments as president and chair of DreamWorks Animation in 2006. The couple has one daughter, born in August 2013 via gestational carrier. Lucas was previously married in 1969 to his ex-wife Marcia Lou Griffin. Marcia is a film editor who won an Academy Award for her work on the first “Star Wars” picture. In 1981, the couple adopted a daughter, Amanda Lucas, then divorced in 1983. Later, as a single father, Lucas adopted two additional children: daughter Katie Lucas in 1988 and son Jett Lucas in 1993. In the 1980s, George was in a relationship with singer Linda Ronstadt after his divorce.

George Lucas’s Net Worth

George Lucas has a long history in the film industry. He has a net worth of $5.4 billion as of November 2023, and he earns a high salary of roughly $500 million per year. Lucas has amassed a considerable fortune through his work as a film director, producer, screenwriter, and entrepreneur.

Inside Love Island winner Olivia Bowens boozy holiday to Ibiza

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, April 1, 2024

LOVE Island's Olivia Bowen has jetted to Ibiza with husband Alex for a sun-soaked getaway.

Last year, the pair welcomed their adorable son Abel and they are currently enjoying some quality couple time on the White Isle.

The Love Island couple - who married in 2018 following their stint on the ITV2 matchmaking show - have headed to the party hotspot to celebrate Alex's 32nd birthday.

Olivia, 29, captured their arrival as they touched down, documenting an Ibiza sign and the 30 degree temperatures.

They then headed straight to a beach bar with trendy wooden seats before getting stuck into colourful cocktails.

Olivia took to her Instagram Stories to document the rainbow coloured drinks which she captioned: "All the daiquiris."

She then captured the stunning sun loungers positioned against a huge pool.

After cuddling up with a pal Olivia, who wore a cute white crochet crop top, shared a kiss with her man.

She showcased a selfie with Alex, sporting a stylish black shirt, planting a kiss on her cheek.

Olivia gave fans a peek at the rest of her outfit, pairing her blouse with a white bikini and matching shorts.

She finished off with a pair of boots and chain necklaces.

Recently, Olivia exclusively revealed to The Sun how she is mum-shamed for wearing bikinis after having a baby.

She has hit back at cruel trolls who slammed her underwear snaps, saying they are "narrow-minded" and "jealous".

Many fans have praised Olivia for sharing body positive posts and for proudly showing off her "real" post-baby body.

Speaking exclusively to The Sun, reality star Olivia said: "I get these awful comments that say that now that I am a mum that I can't pose in lingerie - but it seems very narrow-minded for people to say that sort of thing. 

"I just think you can be a mum, but you can also be you as well. I went to a hen do in St Tropez and it was amazing.

"I felt so disconnected from the 'mum' Olivia.

"But as women, we need to have both and I don't understand why people feel the need to shun a woman wanting to be herself and wanting to be a mum at the same time."

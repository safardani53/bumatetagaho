Creamfields 2019: Line up, set times, weather forecast and can I still buy tickets?

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, February 23, 2024

SWEDISH House Mafia are making their hotly anticipated comeback TONIGHT after a lengthy hiatus and now they headline Creamfields 2019.

Find out which other acts have taken to the stage throughout the weekend as the festival is on it's last day...

When is Creamfields 2019?

The event has officially commenced.

Creamfields has filled up the prime position of the August Bank Holiday in 2019 - having kicked off on Thursday, August 22 and running 'till Sunday, August 25.

The UK dance music festival has been taking place in Daresbury, Cheshire, which is located between Liverpool and Manchester.

The event has a capacity of a whopping 70,000 a day.

When did tickets go on sale for Creamfields 2019?

General sale tickets for Creamfields 2019 were available from 10am on November 30, 2018.

You could buy them through Ticketmaster and Seetickets.

Unfortunately tickets are now sold out.

Camping tickets are ranked from standard to gold - head to the site for an idea of price ranges.

What was the weather forecast during Creamfields?

Fans were in luck as the whole region had plenty of sunshine throughout Saturday. It was very warm everywhere and any cloud was very high in the sky. Maximum temperature 27 Â°C.

As for Sunday, it will stay mostly fine and bright throughout, though it will often be cloudier and less warm than Saturday.

So, there was no need to take a raincoat with you, as the weather held up with plenty of sunshine - perfect for the festival.

What items were not allowed at Creamfields 2019?

The following items were prohibited at the festival:

  • Aerosols (includes deodorant)
  • Air horns
  • Blowtorches
  • Chinese lanterns
  • Firearms and weapons
  • Fireworks or Pyrotechnics
  • Flag poles, however flags are deemed fine to bring
  • Flares
  • Food
  • Gas canisters/cylinders
  • Glass - including perfume and aftershave bottles
  • Hammers
  • Illegal substances (drugs)
  • Laser pens or Laser equipment
  • Masks or other face garments
  • Nitrous Oxide
  • Professional audio annd visual recording equipment
  • Projectiles
  • New psychoactive substances (NPS) and Previous Legal Highs
  • Smoke bombs and canisters
  • Sound system - includes portable speakers
  • Drone or other unmanned aerial vehicles (UAV)
  • Umbrellas (large or golf types)

More on Festivals and music tours

Who is playing at Creamfields 2019?

It was confirmed back in November, 2018 that Swedish House Mafia would headline Creamfields on Sunday, August 25.

A month prior, the band had announced that they would reunite for a series of gigs throughout 2019.

It was further mentioned that a new album was also going to follow, having most recently released their latest song, Underneath It All, back in May.

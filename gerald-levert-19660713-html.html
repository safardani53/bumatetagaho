Gerald Levert (R&amp;B Singer) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Gerald Edward Levert is an American singer, songwriter, producer and actor. Levert is best known for singing with his brother, Sean Levert, and friend Marc Gordon of vocal group LeVert. Levert is also a member of the LSG, a supergroup that includes Keith Sweat, Johnny Gill and himself. Levert is the son of Eddie Levert, who is the lead vocalist of the R&B / soul O'Jays group. He has released nine solo albums, six with LeVert, two with his father Eddie Levert, two with LSG, as well as discovering R&B groups Rude Boys, Men at Large and 1 of the Girls.

Raheem Sterling's cars: Man City winger drives flashy motors worth over 1 million during his short

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, April 25, 2024

WHAT £180k a week can get you...

Man City ace Raheem Sterling has been in blistering form since Pep Guardiola took the helm.

But the flying winger isn't just a fine player, he's also a car connoisseur.

Raheem, 23, has been seen driving a number of expensive cars over the years.

We've spied him turning up to the Etihad Campus in everything from a £500k Bentley to a £12k Smart car.

And totalling up the value of all his rides, we've worked out that the England star has driven at least one million pounds worth of motors in his life.

The most extravagant...

Sterling was spotted in a Bentley GTX 700-4 back in 2016, which is the same car that boss Guardiola drives.

However, being a man who loves the finer things in life, the pacey forward has customised his Bentley to the max.

That's why it's said to be worth a whopping £500k.

The coolest...

Completely unpractical as a city car in Manchester, but class to look at, Sterling's Mercedes G Class SUV is a pleasure on the eye.

And it isn't cheap either, with a base value of around £150k.

We imagine Sterling's is pimped out, meaning the value of his Merc will far exceed the standard rate.

The basic runner...

A car for all his basic needs, like popping to the Trafford Centre, Sterling has favoured a Audi A7 Sportback in the past.

The base price for this vehicle comes in at around £54,940, which is peanuts for Raheem.

Supposedly a lovely drive, it's recognised by car experts as Audi's most innovative car with a dashboard system nearly as hi-tech as the Starship Enterprise.

The footballer's favourite...

Over the years, Raheem has had several Range Rovers, including a white Range Rover Sport, which was valued at around £49k.

He's also been seen in a super-bling Range Rover Evoque Onyx that is somewhere between £45k to over a £100k depending on specifications.

The flashy SUV...

The Porsche Cayenne is one of the most luxurious SUV's in the world.

So, it's no wonder Sterling is happy to pose in front of the gorgeous white Turbo model.

Incredibly, that has a starting price of around £99k.

But it's definitely not one that makes it way out of the garage too often.

The Smart choice...

With footballers beginning to favour electric cars, riding a Smart car is a no-brainer.

Sterling was once snapped arriving to training in the electric motor, which he wrapped in black matte.

It is, by far, his least extravagant ride - with Smart cars retailing at just £12k.

Bruce Wayne's car...

If Bruce Wayne was real, this would definitely be his car of choice.

At the tail-end of last year, Raheem was spotted driving a Bentley Bentayga.

The Bentley's famed SUV, it sells for around £162k - and when it was first released in 2015 it was sold out in its first year of production.

But that didn't stop Raheem from getting his hands on one.

The 'Soccer mom' car...

We should add, if the 'soccer mom' was minted.

Raheem likes space like the best of us, which is why he chose this roomy Audi Q7.

The popular SUV has a starting price of £47,755, which won't dent The Citizens stars' wallet that much.

As a seven-seater, should Sterling should the one-time father want to add to his brood (he has a son called Thiago), this is ideal.

The one that got away...

We wonder if he misses his Mercedes-Benz C 63 AMG?

This was, of course, the car that was reportedly lent to a friend who crashed it back in 2015.

The £125,000 motor was found abandoned on a London street with nitrous cans spotted on the floor of the car.

Does Jesse Spencer Have a Wife? Inside the Actors Love Life

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, May 18, 2024

Jesse Spencer is famously known for his roles as Billy Kennedy on the Australian soap opera Neighbours and Captain Matthew Casey on the American drama Chicago Fire.

But despite being a famed star, he prefers to keep his personal life away from the spotlight. Because of that, his fans are only more curious to know if he has a wife.

Is Jesse Spencer Married?

If you are one of Spencer’s fans and wondering to know if he is married, here is your answer — he is married!

He married his longtime girlfriend, Kali Woodruff Carr, on June 27, 2020.

Spencer with his spouse Kali Woodruff Carr at the Lyric Opera in 2018. (Photo: Twitter)

The wedding was an intimate affair with only selected friends and family members in attendance.

The Chicago Fire star confirmed the news to The Daily Telegraph in October 2020 and described their special day as their “silver lining to 2020.”

He further revealed that the intimate ceremony took place in his wife's hometown of Neptune Beach, Florida, on the same day as her grandparents’ wedding anniversary.

“Despite a spike in COVID, a venue change, a Saharan dust plume, not to mention a torrential thunderstorm, we made it across the line on our wedding day, and I’m a very lucky man,” Spencer told the tabloid.

Jesse Spencer and Wife Are Happy to Be Together

Spencer and his wife began dating in 2014 after meeting at the Chicago music festival.

Following that, the actor got on bended knees to propose to her during a week-long hike in the Peruvian Andes in 2019 — and became engaged.

During the aforementioned conversation with The Daily Telegraph, Spencer gushed about his relationship with his wife and how elated they were to be together.

“We’re very happy. She is my port in every storm, and we’re already excited to be moving on to the next phase of our lives,” he stated.

He later praised his bride by describing her as smart, pretty, and “way ahead of her years.”

In November 2021, Spencer and Carr were also reported to be expecting their first child together. A Twitter handle under the name @onechicagonews broke the news.

Who Is Jesse Spencer’s Wife?

Spencer’s wife, Carr, is an independent woman who is a research scientist by profession.

According to Research Gate’s website, she has been working as a research specialist at the Department of Psychology at Northwestern University since July 2018.

She has a Ph.D. in communication sciences & disorders and MA in communication sciences. She also has a bachelor's degree in psychology and music performance.

Jesse Spencer’s Past Relationships

Even though Spencer has been tight-lipped about his personal life over the years, a few details about his love life is public knowledge.

For instance, he was previously engaged to actress Jennifer Morrison. 

The duo first met in March 2004 at Vancouver International Airport when they were on their way to film the pilot episode of the medical drama House.

They began dating shortly after in July 2004 and got engaged during their vacation in Paris at the Eiffel Tower during Christmas 2006.

However, they called off their engagement in August 2007.

Spencer later dated British actress and singer Louise Griffiths from 2008 to 2010 and Brazilian big wave surfer Maya Gabeira from 2010 to 2013.

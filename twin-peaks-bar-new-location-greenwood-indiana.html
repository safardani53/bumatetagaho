Twin Peaks announces major celebration after launching '100th location' and customers will love the

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, September 28, 2024

A POPULAR sports bar chain has debuted its 100th location with a grand opening party.

Twin Peaks introduced its latest addition to the public on Friday in Greenwood, Indiana – a suburb immediately south of Indianapolis.

The celebration included a live DJ, games and prizes, and an opportunity to take photos with two Indianapolis Colts players, wide receiver Malik Turner and linebacker Segun Olubi.

The new location will have its first normal day of business on Saturday, Twin Peaks announced in a press release earlier this week.

It will be open from 11am to midnight Sunday through Thursday and from 11am to 1am on Fridays and Saturdays.

Customers can also come get $6 discounted appetizers for the location's happy hour, which will run from 2-6pm.

"Consistency is the key to success and our entire team has worked hard every single day to get us to this milestone moment," Twin Peaks CEO Joe Hummel said in the release.

"With the Indy 500, Colts, Pacers and local college and high school teams, there’s no better spot than the Indianapolis area to have the number-one sports bar’s 100th location!"

Ahead of the opening, Twin Peaks – which is known for its rustic lodge vibes – added more than a dozen new drink options to its menu.

It now includes a new martini section, six new tequilas, and a mezcal option.

The chain has expanded a lot this year. It's already opened new locations in Tennessee, Arizona, Missouri, and Florida.

There are also plans to open restaurants in Columbus, Ohio, and the border town McAllen, Texas, later this year.

"We’re energized by the way we’ve begun 2023 and looking forward to even more impressive results throughout the year," Hummel said.

Twin Peaks is not the only chain that's currently expanding.

BJ's Wholesale Club will open its first location in Tennessee on June 14.

Myleene Klass looks incredible as she poses topless to show off her tan for bikini shoot

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, June 21, 2024

TV favourite Myleene Klass became the nation's sweetheart when she stripped off to her bikini in 2006 for I'm A Celeb.

And judging by her latest post, the 44 year old mum of three certainly hasn't lost her mojo, after going topless to show off her tan for her bikini shoot.

Starting at 5am for her glam-over ahead of the shoot, Myleene, who shot to fame with pop group Hear'Say in 2001, posted a video of her time on location.

In one segment, Myleene showed fans her make shift changing room, labelling it a 'tardis' while accompanying it with the Dr Who theme tune.

And in another segment of her Instagram video, she is seen holding up nude invisible knickers and captioned the post: "If you know, you know."

But it wasn't that long ago that the radio star was jumping around and frolicking in the Maldives, treating fans to her toned body.

And her fans were quick to praise the star. One said: "Amazing as always."

And another wrote: "You're incredible ❤️."

Myleene spent the festive period in the Maldives with her partner Simon Motson, their son Apollo, two and her two daughters Ava, 14 and Hero, 11.

Myleene had been sharing and documenting her family trip on Instagram. And one milestone she was keen to reveal was teaching her son how to swim.

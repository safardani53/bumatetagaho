Gwen Stefani and Miranda Lambert: Both Pregnant?!

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, March 30, 2024

Everyone wants Blake Shelton and Gwen Stefani to get married and have babies.

The tabloids might want it the most, because every few weeks the happy couple graces their front covers with all sorts of dramatic headlines:

OMG They’re Pregnant!

OMG They’re Getting Married And Having a Baby!

OMG They’re Doing IVF!

Life & Style’s October 17th cover story claims that not only are Shelton and his (allegedly) pregnant bride walking down the aisle, so is Shelton’s ex-wife, Miranda Lambert.

“Blake Shelton is set to become a first-time dad (and third-time husband!) with Gwen Stefani by his side," the story reads.

“Over the past few months” Stefani “has been planning a blowout Christmas wedding and trying to get pregnant.”

In fact, “friends believe she already could be pregnant," a source claims.

“She’s been eating a lot of carbs, which she never eats except when she’s pregnant."

Stefani has three boys with ex-husband, Gavin Rossdale – Kingston, Zuma and Apollo.

She “wants a baby girl after years of feeling outnumbered at home.”

I’m sure her sons would feel super after reading that.

And since Stefani has already done the whole not-pregnant-at-the-altar thing, “expecting a baby wouldn’t make her postpone her nuptials.” 

The thought of being knocked up and single doesn’t sit well with Stefani

“She wouldn’t want to be an unwed mother at the time of the baby’s birth, so she’ll definitely be marrying Blake before then," the source added.

Stefani "has no problem walking down the aisle while pregnant.

"She sees how amazing Blake is with her kids and she told him she wants to give him his own child."

In short, Shelton and Stefani are "actively trying to start a family.

"They’ve never been happier or more in love."

As for Lambert?  The 32-year-old country singer has been dating fellow musician Anderson East since last December, and things might be getting super-serious.

Lambert canceled the final three dates of her tour, citing "vocal issues."

Insiders, however, believe Lambert and East, 28, are expecting a baby together.

“Miranda’s had a glow about her, and friends believe she’ll be announcing that she’s going to be having a baby very soon," a source told Life & Style.

“Her career has been a priority until now [but] Miranda is always telling friends about how Anderson would make a great dad."

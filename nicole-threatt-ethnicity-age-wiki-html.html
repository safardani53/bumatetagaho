Nicole Threatt Wiki Reveals Age, Ethnicity, Nationality - Essential Details On Dr. Dre's Wife

1. Nicole Threatt's Bio: Ethnicity, Nationality

Nicole was born on 1 January 1970 in the United States, the bonafide American national posses a mixed ethnicity. Her maiden name is not Nicole Threatt but adopted the name from her previous marriage to retired NBA star Sedale Threatt.

However, she now addresses herself as Nicole Young after getting married to Dr. Dre, whose real name is Andre Romelle Young is one of the wealthiest Afro-American ethnicity rappers of all time.

Despite being married to one of the most known faces in the rap scene, someone who is considered to be the man behind superstar rappers Eminem, Snoop Dogg and 50 Cent, Nicole continues to lead a secretive lifestyle shunning the limelight.

With no whatsoever information about her upbringing, Nicole's educational background is for all to see as she herself, has admitted that she is a law graduate. However, she doesn’t practice law and has chosen to be a dedicated housewife, mother, and a socialite.

2. Romantic Letter From Dr. Dre Stole Her Heart

Before Dre, Nicole was married to now-retired NBA player Sedale Threatt. Nicole married her NBA star husband in 1992 when she was just 22 years of age.

Nicole was still married to Sedale when she met Dre in 1995. It was after his first meeting with Nicole the iconic gangster penned a love letter to her.

In the letter, Dre asked Nicole to leave Sedale for him. The opening of the letter read,

Hopefully you doing well and you realized you need to quit f****** with Sedale and come home to your doctor. I will take care of you baby girl.

After the meeting, Nicole's love life showed signs of cracks, and after her subsequent divorce, it seemed that Dr. Dre's charm offensive had worked on then 25-year-old Nicole.

After the finalization of her divorce in 1995 with Sedale, she tied the knots with Dre in 1996, leading to speculations that the love letter was the reason behind her divorce with Sedale. Nevertheless, she moved on from Sedale to Dr. Dre, and with the pairs relation going strong as ever, the divorce decision could have been one of the best choices she had ever taken.

3. Her Husband Dr. Dre's Net Worth

Despite leading a furtive life away from the spotlight, one of the significant things known about Nicole is that she had a career as an attorney. However, she stopped practicing upon her marriage to Dre.

Moreover, who needs a job when your husband is a hip-hop star with a net worth of over $800 million.

Im A Celebrity South Africa final date revealed as ITV announce movie-length episode to crown jun

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 9, 2024

I'M A Celebrity South Africa's final date has revealed - and it's sooner than you think.

Although the ITV spin-off show has only just hit our screens, viewers don't have long to wait until the epic finale to see who is crowned winner.

Final schedules have confirmed that the final instalment will air on May 12 - just over two weeks from now.

The episode will be 90 minutes long, airing between 9pm and 10.30pm.

I'm A Celebrity South Africa premiered on Monday and has already seen a number of famous faces take on some gruesome Bushtucker trials.

Jordan Banjo and Janice Dickinson have already had take part in the dreaded eating trial, while last night Georgia Toffolo braved the Dreaded Drain before being welcomed into the main camp.

Boxing champion Amir Khan, former Coronation Street Star Helen Flanagan, TV presenter Carol Vorderman, Olympic athlete Fatima Whitbread, former royal butler Paul Burrell, ex-England cricketer Phil Tufnell, music legend Shaun Ryder, Jordan and Janice were the original celebrities to enter camp.

They got a shock when TV nutritionist Gillian McKeith later joined them as she jumped out of a box.

Last night viewers saw singer Myleene Klass, Made In Chelsea's Georgia ‘Toff’ Toffolo and Corrie's Andy Whyment become the latest arrivals.

However the main camp picked Georgia to do the trial, meaning that they was given a place over there, instead of the sister camp.

Patrick Schwarzenegger & Taylor Burns’ Spring Break PDA — PICS

Can you believe this is happening again? Poor Miley!

From the looks of these new photos, Patrick had more fun without Miley than anyone else realized on Sunday, March 15 in Cabo San Lucas, Mexico. Surprisingly, his extra fun was with his own ex-girlfriend, Taylor!

In the new photos, Patrick and a bikini-clad Taylor are seen wrapping their arms around each other tightly. As their one-on-one time continues, the two get very flirty and Taylor even playfully steals his fedora and wears it around the bar they are hanging out in.

Patrick & Taylor Dated For Over A Year, Split In 2014

Although it’s unknown when or why they split, it’s clear that Patrick and Taylor decided to go their separate ways somewhere around spring/summer 2014. In fact, the last time they were photographed together was in May 2014, according to Just Jared Jr., and neither one of them look very happy in those old pictures.

The first time they were photographed together was in March 2013 when they were caught locking lips in the ocean on a romantic vacation, reports Us Weekly.

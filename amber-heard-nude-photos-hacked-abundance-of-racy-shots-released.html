Amber Heard Nude Photos Hacked, Abundance of Racy Shots Released Online

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, May 13, 2024

Amber Heard has become the latest Hollywood hacker victim.

In a wide-sweeping new release of naked photos by an unknown, perverted culprit on Reddit and 4chan, stars such as Rihanna, Kim Kardashian, Hope Solo, Vanessa Hudgens and Hayden Panettiere have seen their privacy invaded.

And now Heard has joined the unfortunate crowd, with over FIFTY topless images of the actress hitting the Internet against her will.

We will not post the lewd images here (out of respect), but the hacking of Heard is the most prolific since this same criminal released Jennifer Lawrence nude and Kate Upton nude pictures a few weeks at the beginning of this scandal.

In one photo Heard is holding up a sign that is very clearly a message to her husband, Johnny Depp. It refers to him as “Tonto.”

No word from anyone in Heard’s camp yet, but the FBI says its investigation is ongoing.

Earlier this weekend, Gabrielle Union was also hacked, with the actress responding as angrily as anyone victim to date.

“I can’t help but to be reminded that since the dawn of time women and children, specifically women of color, have been victimized, and the power over their own bodies taken from them,” she wrote via statement. “These atrocities against women and children continue worldwide.”

The Being Mary Jane star reached out personally to the FBI in response to the criminal act and concluded:

“For anyone out there also being affected by these and other hacking and hate crimes – We send our love, support and prayers. We have done nothing wrong.”

Boss Hogg Outlawz is an American hip-hop group from Houston, Texas, founded and directed by Slim Thug. The original team included Le $, Young Von, C. Ward, J-Dawg, Killa Kyleon, Sir Daily, Lil Ray, Young Black, Dre Day, PJ & MUG. The group debuted in 2001 when Slim Thug and ESG released a collaboration album Boss Hogg Outlaws. In 2004, Slim gathered a group of rappers from Houston and released Boss Hogg's debut album Outlawz Boyz-n-Blue. In 2006, the band signed with Koch Records and released their second album titled Serve & Collect in 2007. The following year saw the launch of Back by Blockular Demand: Serve & Collect II. The group returned in 2011 with their third album Serve & Collect although C. Ward and Killa Kyleon left the group and did not appear on the album.

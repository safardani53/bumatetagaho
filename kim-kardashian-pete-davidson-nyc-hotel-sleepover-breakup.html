Kim Kardashian and ex Pete Davidson 'shared a secret sleepover in her NYC hotel room' two months aft

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, June 3, 2024

KIM Kardashian and her ex-Pete Davidson "shared a secret sleepover in her New York City hotel room" two months after their breakup. 

A source exclusively told The U.S. Sun that Kim made a discreet trip to the Big Apple to see her comedian ex-boyfriend while he was filming Bupkis - right after she returned from Paris Fashion Week.  

"Pete visited Kim at her room and the next day Kim was seen sneaking out a back entrance of the hotel in a disguise and hopped in a waiting Escalade to visit Pete on Staten Island," a source said. 

The National Enquirer also reported on the meeting.  

A rep for Kim did not return The U.S. Sun's request for comment.  

FRIENDLY EXES 

The pair have remained friendly after a very amicable breakup and have even reportedly been texting each other in recent weeks.

Kim and Pete split in August after nine months of dating. 

At the time of the break-up, it was reported that the Kardashians star and former SNL funnyman had decided to just be friends. 

"They have a lot of love and respect for each other," one insider says, "but found that the long distance and their demanding schedules made it really difficult to maintain a relationship." 

PETE AND KIM 

The couple met on the set of Saturday Night Live and shared a kissing scene during an Aladdin magic carpet ride skit.

"It was a stage kiss, but it was still a little zing," Kim said on the Not Skinny But Not Fat podcast in April. 

They started dating in November 2021, and a source told E! News at the time: "Pete has told her he doesn't want to see anyone else. 

"She is telling some people they aren't super serious but she isn't seeing anyone else... She's trying to not make a big deal about it but is super into him." 

After they began dating, Davidson appeared on episodes of The Kardashians. 

"I wasn't even thinking, like, Oh my god, I'm going to be in a relationship with him," Kardashian said on the show. 

"I was just thinking, like, 'Heard about this BDE, need to get out there.' I was just basically DTF." 

In season two of the family's Hulu show, Kim can be heard gushing about Pete in several of the episodes- though he noticeably has not yet made an on-camera appearance.

The musician - who has four kids with Kim - repeatedly targeted the TV star and her now ex Pete Davidson after they began dating in October 2021.

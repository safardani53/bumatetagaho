Harry Styles: How to get tickets to 2023 Love On Tour

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, September 10, 2024

HARRY Styles has announced his huge Love on Tour will continue next year, 2023.

With a huge run of European stadium shows announced for next summer this is how you can get tickets.

The current Love on Tour tour has been a huge success, and so we can expect 2023's set of dates to be as popular.

He'll be performing at a number of European venues across Spain, Portugal, Ireland, Germany and Poland during the run.

UK fans will be able to see him 13 - 14 June in London's Wembley Stadium, and 20 June in Cardiff.

General sale tickets will go on sale September 2 at 10am, demand is expected to be huge so don't hang about.

Tickets are available from Ticketmaster here.

Is OVC Host David Venable Married Or Are Gay Talks True? Bio Reveals

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 7, 2024

An irresistible personality whose happy dance and commentaries have everyone hooked to his show—David Venable is an American television personality. He is most popularly known as the host of the QVC Network's In The Kitchen with David.

Selling millions of copies, he is also the author of three cookbooks: Comfort Foods That Take You Home: A Cookbook (2012), Back Around the Table: An "In the Kitchen with David" Cookbook from QVC's Resident Foodie (2014), and Comfort Food Shortcuts: An "In the Kitchen with David" Cookbook (2018).

David Venable Bio: Raised By Single Mother Who Introduced Him To Cooking

Born on NovemNovember 124, David Venable is a native of Charlotte, North Carolina. He grew up in a family of five with his mother, Sarah, and two siblings: a brother Chip and a sister whose name remains undisclosed.

He and his siblings were raised by a single mother who taught them how to cook as she needed help around the kitchen. Among the three, David was the one who always made his way back to the kitchen.

For his college, with the intention of pursuing a career in hard news, he attended the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he graduated with a degree in journalism.

David Venable’s Career Change From News Anchor To TV Show Host At QVC

After graduating, the towering man with a height of 6 feet 6 inches started his career in the television news industry. He worked as an anchor and reporter for the mid-day news in West Virginia and Altoona, Pennsylvania.

During his time as a news anchor at Altoona, he landed a vacancy at QVC after he stumbled onto an ad in a local newspaper. Soon after working six years as a broadcast journalist, he applied for and was selected at QVC in 1993.

He was hired to promote epicure food products for QVC. And soon, with David’s charming persona, outstanding commentaries, and famous happy dance, he captured the hearts of millions. He has been at QVC ever since.

In 2014, he sold more than $250 million worth of egg poachers, frozen crab cakes, and backyard smokers. Not only that, he is so well-loved among his viewers at QVC that the show gets thousands of calls from customers who simply want a chance to speak with the host.

David Venable’s Salary And Net Worth Revealed

Having been a QVC host for over two decades, he has accumulated a respectable fortune from the network, and then there are the profits from his widely popular book sales.

As a QVC on-air host anchoring the show without scripted commentaries, he earns an estimated average salary of $74,690/year. And all that adds up to his massive net worth of $1 million.

A Look Into David Venable’s Personal Life Which He Likes To Keep Private

Venable doesn’t make comments on his personal life as openly as he shares about the food products. When asked about his personal life, he smiles kindly and politely requests that his private life be kept confidential.

But his statement came with a cost. Due to his lowkey love life, fans began speculating that he was gay. Not only that, he has also been the subject of unflattering blog posts about his body and his sex life.

Today host Savannah Guthrie embarrasses husband Mike Feldman on live TV by revealing shocking bedroo

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, February 24, 2024

TODAY Show host Savannah Guthrie has embarrassed her husband, Michael Feldman, on live TV.

As Savannah, 51, and her Today co-hosts were talking about a loud noise, she revealed a shocking bedroom secret about her spouse.

On Friday's show, Savannah, Craig Melvin, and Hoda Kotb talked about how an asteroid the size of a truck nearly hit Earth.

The asteroid came within 22,000 miles of the earth's surface.

"This happened last night, by the tip of South America," Craig, 43, explained.

"Scientists are calling it 'one of the closest approaches to Earth ever recorded,' but they added there was no impact because NASA's Impact Assessment System, yes, that is a thing, it's called SCOUT, had ruled out a direct hit."

"But while you were sleeping last night...." Craig said.

Savannah then delivered a jab at her husband by saying: "I think I heard it. Or it was probably snoring next to me."

Hoda, 58, laughed and Craig said: "That sounds about right."

Savannah then laughed and moved on to the next topic.

LET IT SINK IN

Savannah has revealed a lot about her husband while on TV.

Back in November, she revealed why she and Mike "fought over the weekend."

The mother-of-two admitted to her co-hosts that she can only talk about it because he was "currently overseas."

The hosts talked about how singer P!nk got candid about fighting with her husband over leaving dishes in the sink overnight to soak.

After the clip of the singer played, Savannah opened up about a similar situation.

"This is funny, and I can actually open up about this because my husband is overseas right now," she said.

"We actually had a fight about this this weekend."

"What happened?" asked Hoda and Jacob Soboroff.

"He was like 'I did the dishes' and then the dishes were in the sink and I'm like, 'Let's bring them across the finish line and put them in the dishwasher," she said.

Hoda clapped and laughed at her response.

"How do you suppose that gets into the dishwasher? It doesn't crawl in there," she said.

Her co-host Craig spoke up and said: "What if the dish actually did need to soak? What if the grime was so bad-?"

"Well then, you know what? Get it across the finish line the next day," Savannah said.

"I don't think any dish needs to soak overnight," Hoda said.

TMI!

Savannah opens up a lot about her husband on the show and on social media.

And mostly it is not about fighting with him.

Back in August, she posted a screenshot of her private text messages with Michael.

Savannah posted to her Instagram stories that she woke up late and thought she wasn't going to make it to her job on time.

She recounted the whole process including getting her hair and make-up done quickly and walking onto set with five minutes to spare.

After making it to the Today stage on time, she revealed a text she received from her husband that morning.

"You made it AND you look hot," it read.

To that, Savannah replied: "Awww I love you baby."

The Today Show co-host captioned the screenshot with: "Nomination for husband of the year" and arrows pointing toward the text.

She then added emojis with hearts around them and tagged him.

Savannah revealed the pet name she calls Michael when she wrote: "My schmoopie @feldmike."
